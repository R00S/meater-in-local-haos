## Research notes

Sources: FAOSTAT 2023-2024; Cameroonian Ministry of Livestock, Fisheries and Animal Industries (2020 Annual Note);
WorldMapper plantain data; World Population Review palm oil rankings; Statbase.org meat data; academic cuisine sources.
Key facts: Cameroon = world's #1 plantain producer (~4 M t/yr, 150 kg/capita); 14th cassava (6.66 M t, 2024);
13th palm oil (320,000 t, 2023); total meat 418,231 t (2024, FAOSTAT); artisanal fishing = 86% of fisheries output.
Signature national dish = ndolé (bitter leaf + groundnut stew). Poulet DG = iconic festive chicken.
Far North = millet/sorghum belt. Forest South/SW = eru, njangsa, mbongo. Anglophone SW/NW = eru, achu.
