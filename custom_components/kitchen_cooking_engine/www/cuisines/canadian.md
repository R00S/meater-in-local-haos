Key figures used:
- Chicken: 35.35 kg/capita (Ag Canada 2022), Pork: 19.99 kg/capita, Beef: 17.5 kg/capita
- Quebec produces ~72% of world maple syrup (PPAQ 2024-25)
- Canada: world #3 wheat exporter (35M tonnes 2024), world #1 canola producer (21.8M tonnes 2025)
- Atlantic lobster: highest-value fishery, CAD $2.8B shellfish (DFO 2023)
- Alberta: ~43% of national cattle herd (10.9M head, Jan 2025)
- Canada dairy: 15.3 kg cheese/capita, 3.8 kg butter/capita, 58.7L fluid milk/capita (2023)
- Canada: world #1 mustard seed exporter, 192K tonnes harvested 2024 (Saskatchewan 75%)
- Wild blueberries: NS/NB — Atlantic Canada world's largest wild blueberry region
- PEI: 20.4% of Canada's record 127M hundredweight potato crop (2024)
