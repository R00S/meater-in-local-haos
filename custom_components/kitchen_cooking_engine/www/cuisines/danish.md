Protein consumption (carcass weight): pork ~57.9 kg/cap (2023, FAOSTAT/Helgi), poultry ~22–25 kg/cap, beef ~15 kg/cap. Fish/seafood ~20–25 kg/cap total (Eurofish). Dairy: drinking milk ~81–82 kg/cap, cheese ~28 kg/cap, butter ~4.25 kg/cap (CLAL 2022). Grain: wheat 87.6 kg/cap, rye ~20–25 kg/cap, rice 9.44 kg/cap.
