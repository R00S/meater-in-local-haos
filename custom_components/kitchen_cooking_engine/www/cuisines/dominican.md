## Research notes

Sources: FAO GIEWS Dominican Republic country briefs 2023–2025; USDA FAS Grain and Feed Annual Dominican Republic 2022/2023; Helgi Library Dominican Republic per capita meat/rice/dairy; World Population Review meat consumption; World Population Review avocado consumption 2022; IANAS Food and Nutrition Security Dominican Republic 2025; RVO Dutch Agricultural Sector Report Dominican Republic 2023; Dominican Republic Ministry of Agriculture statistics; culturally verified via Spanish-language Dominican culinary sources.

Protein consumption: chicken ~32–35 kg/cap, pork 16.5 kg/cap (2022, all-time high per Helgi Library), beef ~7–9 kg/cap, total meat ~57.8 kg/cap 2022 (FAO). Rice: 50–75 kg/cap (USDA FAS 2022–2023). Avocado: 49 kg/cap (2022, world's highest, World Population Review). Vegetable oil: ~12–14 kg/cap. Sazón market: $1.09 billion USD 2024.
