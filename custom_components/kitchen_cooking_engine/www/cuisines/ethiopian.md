Key statistics: Chicken meat 0.5 kg/cap (2023, Helgi Library, lowest globally). Total meat ~8–10 kg/cap. Ethiopian Orthodox fasting ~140 days/year (vegan diet). Teff: 50M+ Ethiopians eat daily. Wheat 6.5M MT production 2025. Coffee 495 000 MT 2022/23 (Africa's largest; USDA FAS). Honey 98 000 MT 2023 (Africa #1, world #10; Ethiopian Monitor). Ethiopia milk 12 billion liters/year; 75% processed into butter, 25% into ayib.
