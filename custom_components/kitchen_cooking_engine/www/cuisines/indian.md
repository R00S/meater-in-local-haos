Key findings: India is the world's largest spice producer, consumer, and exporter. Fish (13 kg/capita) is the #1 animal protein by weight, followed by eggs. Ghee, paneer, and yogurt (dahi) are the iconic dairy trio. Mustard oil defines East/North Indian cooking; coconut oil defines South Indian cooking. Turmeric and cumin are used in virtually every dish. Sugar consumption 19-20 kg/capita; salt 5-8 kg/capita. Cashews are the most consumed nut (India is world's largest producer).
