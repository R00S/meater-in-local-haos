## Research notes

Sources: FAO Food Balance Sheet 2022 Kenya; USDA Kenya Grain and Feed Annual 2022; Kenya National Bureau of Statistics 2022 Statistical Abstract; KNBS National Agriculture Production Report 2024; dairy data: ~110 liters milk/capita/year (highest in East Africa); meat per capita: beef ~4.1 kg, poultry ~1.7 kg, goat/sheep ~1.2 kg, fish ~2–3 kg, legumes/beans ~12–14 kg; grains: maize ~80 kg/capita, wheat ~40 kg/capita, rice ~21 kg/capita. Top cooking oil: palm oil (most used, imported), sunflower oil (locally grown). Key identity dishes: nyama choma, ugali, sukuma wiki, pilau, githeri, chapati, mandazi, mukimo, mursik.
