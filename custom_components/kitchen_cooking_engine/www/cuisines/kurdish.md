## Research notes

Sources: FAO Food Balance Sheets Iraq/Turkey/Iran/Syria 2022; Kurdish culinary traditions documented in Naomi Duguid "Taste of Persia" and multiple regional food surveys; USDA Global Agricultural Information Network reports for Iraq (sheep/lamb dominant protein), Turkey (poultry #1), Iran (rice 37 kg/capita, wheat 155 kg/capita); key Kurdish dishes: kofte (meatballs), dolma (stuffed vegetables), pacha (head and trotter stew), kofta biryani, tashrib (bread-soup), masgouf-influenced fish dishes (Iraqi Kurdish), mantı-style dumplings, harisa (wheat and meat porridge), qeymeh (split pea stew with lamb). Kurdish region spans SE Turkey, N Iraq, NW Iran, N Syria — agricultural zone producing wheat, barley, lentils, chickpeas, pomegranate, walnuts, lamb, goat.
