## Research notes

Sources: FAO Food Balance Sheets Laos 2022; World Bank Laos food data 2022; Lao Statistics Bureau household food consumption survey 2020; USDA Global Agricultural Information Network Laos 2022; key facts: sticky glutinous rice ~175 kg/capita (world's highest per capita consumption), fresh water fish dominant protein (Mekong river basin ~50-70 kg/capita fish), pork ~8 kg/capita, chicken ~5 kg/capita, buffalo and beef small but culturally important; padaek (fermented fish paste) consumed ~20 kg/capita/yr; major vegetables: morning glory, eggplant, banana flower, bamboo shoots, papaya; Lao coffee (Bolaven Plateau arabica) famous; key dishes: laap/larb (national dish), tam mak houng (papaya salad), ping pa (grilled fish), mok pa (fish in banana leaf), or lam (herb stew), khao piak sen (noodle soup), khao niaw (sticky rice).
