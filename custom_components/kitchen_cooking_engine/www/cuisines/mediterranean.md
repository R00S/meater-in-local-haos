## Research notes

Sources: FAO Food Balance Sheets; Greece lamb/goat ~7 kg/capita, Spain poultry ~27 kg, Italy pasta/wheat dominant; Mediterranean Fish species: European anchovy and sardine are top catches by volume (FAO Mediterranean fisheries). Vegetable consumption very high across region: Turkey ~244 kg/capita total vegetables, Italy ~114 kg/capita; top vegetables: tomato (#1), onion (#2), potato, cucumber. Key signature grain: wheat (bread, pasta, couscous), rice (paella, risotto); local grains: durum wheat (Italy, North Africa), barley (Spain, Morocco, Turkey). Olive oil: THE defining cooking fat; cheese: feta (sheep/goat, Greece), pecorino (Italy), manchego (Spain), halloumi (Cyprus). Key spices: oregano, thyme, rosemary, saffron, mint, bay leaves, za'atar (Levant), sumac, capers. Harissa: Tunisia/North Africa.
