Sources: FAO Food Balance India 2022; NSSO Household Consumption Survey India 2022-23; ICAR livestock census India; academic papers on Mughlai culinary history (Collingham 2006 "Curry: A Tale of Cooks and Conquerors"; Salma Husain "The Mughal Feast"); fisheries statistics CIFRI India (Central Inland Fisheries Research Institute); APEDA (Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority) spice production data India.
