## Research notes

Sources: Helsedirektoratet "Utviklingen i norsk kosthold 2024"; Norkost 4 dietary survey 2022–23; SSB grain statistics 2025; Statistics Norway (SSB) dietary statistics; FAO food balance Norway; Norwegian Fishermen's Association; NIBIO grain and forage crops report; Norway Seafood Council (NSC) salmon statistics. Key stats: Norway world's largest farmed Atlantic salmon producer (~1.5 million tonnes/year); pork 25 kg/cap/year (most consumed meat), chicken ~22 kg/cap, beef ~14 kg/cap (Helsedirektoratet 2023); most consumed vegetables: carrot, onion, tomato, lettuce (Norkost 4); grain production: barley 690k tonnes (most), wheat 263k tonnes, oats 244k tonnes, rye 17k tonnes (SSB 2025); Jarlsberg most exported Norwegian cheese; brunost/geitost uniquely Norwegian product; Norway among world's highest cardamom consumers per capita.
