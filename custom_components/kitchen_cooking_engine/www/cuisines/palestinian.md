## Research notes

Sources: Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics (PCBS) Agricultural Census 2021; FAO FAOSTAT food balance sheets Palestine; Al Jazeera "Infographic: Palestine's olive industry" 2021; ANERA Palestinian agricultural reports; Heinrich-Böll-Stiftung "Agriculture in Palestine" 2021; Agroecology Fund Palestine reports; Wikipedia: "Agriculture in Palestine"; Oxford Companion to Food (Alan Davidson) — Levantine cuisine entries; Anissa Helou "Feast: Food of the Islamic World"; Joudie Kalla "Palestine on a Plate"; Reem Kassis "The Palestinian Table". Key stats: ~10 million olive trees cover ~50% of Palestinian cultivated land; ~39,600 tonnes olive oil/year (2019 PCBS); olives economically central; figs, grapes, carob, prickly pear traditional heritage crops; wheat, barley, lentils, chickpeas locally grown; Awassi fat-tailed sheep breed is native Palestinian/Levantine sheep producing jameed milk.
