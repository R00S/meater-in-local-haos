## Research notes

Sources: GUS Poland (Central Statistical Office) food consumption data 2020–2022; FAO FAOSTAT Poland 2021; Eurostat agricultural statistics Poland; Polish Pork Industry Association — Poland top-5 EU pork producer; KOWR (National Agricultural Support Centre) Poland cereals data; Wikipedia Polish cuisine; Adam Kilian, Maria Disslowa "Polish Cookery"; Anne Applebaum various Polish food writings; UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage Poland (Christmas barszcz tradition, oscypek cheese PDO). Key facts: Poland pork ~65 kg/capita/year gross weight (GUS 2021), among Europe's highest; Poland top EU poppy seed producer (Łódź, Opole regions); Poland #2 EU buckwheat producer; oscypek smoked sheep cheese PDO from Podhale (Tatra mountains); dill (koperek) the national herb; marjoram (majeranek) unique to Polish sausage tradition.
