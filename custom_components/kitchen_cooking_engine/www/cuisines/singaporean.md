## Research notes

Sources: Singapore Food Statistics 2022 (SFA); worldmetrics.org Singapore food industry 2022;
SFA media release 2023; SFA 30-by-30 local farming report; Barramundi Asia aquaculture.
Per capita 2022: chicken ~36 kg/cap/yr (~45% of meat), pork ~28 kg/cap/yr (~35%), seafood ~22 kg/cap/yr;
eggs ~390/person/yr (~24 kg); beef/mutton small share; total meat ~80 kg/cap/yr.
Local farming: SFA-licensed farms grow leafy greens (chye sim, kai lan, lettuce), barramundi, grouper,
tilapia, eggs; "30 by 30" national food security goal; Sky Greens, Sustenir Agriculture, Apollo Aquaculture.
Pork dominates Singaporean-Chinese hawker culture; Hainanese chicken rice is national dish.
