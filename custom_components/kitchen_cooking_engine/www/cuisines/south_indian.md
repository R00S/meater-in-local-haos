## Research notes

Sources: NSSO Nutritional Intake in India 2022-24; Handbook on Fisheries Statistics 2022; Times of India;
South First nutrition report; NSSO 68th round; The Hindu Businessline; FAOSTAT India;
Kerala protein: ~73.9 g/cap/day; ~40% from animal (fish #1, beef #2, chicken #3); beef dominates Kerala;
Tamil Nadu: chicken #1 animal protein, fish #2; 18.1% from animal sources;
Karnataka: chicken + fish; moderate beef; ~20% animal protein;
Kerala cattle slaughter: 153,000 tonnes/yr (3x Tamil Nadu) — beef dominant in Kerala;
Fish: Kerala and Tamil Nadu among highest marine fish consuming states in India;
Local crops: coconut (Kerala #1 crop), banana, tapioca (kappa), jackfruit (Kerala state fruit),
black pepper and cardamom (Western Ghats), curry leaf (endemic to South India);
Coconut oil: dominant cooking fat in Kerala; gingelly (sesame) oil in Tamil Nadu.
