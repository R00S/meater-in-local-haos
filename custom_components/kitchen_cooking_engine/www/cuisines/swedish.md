## Research notes

Sources: SCB (Statistics Sweden) meat consumption data 2022, Livsmedelsverket (Swedish Food Agency)
dietary surveys, Jordbruksverket annual statistics, Swedish culinary heritage literature
(Gastronomisk Kalender, Vår Mat, Husmanskost by Leif Mannerström).

Protein consumption kg/capita/year (2022, SCB): pork ~35, poultry ~26, beef ~23, fish (total) ~11.
Game is culturally significant beyond its consumption volume — elk and reindeer define Swedish
hunting tradition and Sami heritage respectively.

Signature dishes surveyed: köttbullar med lingon (meatball mix), gravlax, inlagd sill,
fläskpannkaka, kalops (beef stew), wallenbergare, nässelsoppa, äggakaka,
revbensspjäll, Janssons frestelse (anchovy/potato gratin), laxpudding.
