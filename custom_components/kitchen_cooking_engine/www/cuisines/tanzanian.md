## Research notes

Sources: FAO FAOSTAT Tanzania food balance 2021; USDA GAIN Tanzania Grain and Feed 2022;
Tanzania National Bureau of Statistics 2020 household consumption survey;
WorldFish Tanzania aquaculture report; African Development Bank Tanzania livestock 2022;
Zanzibar Commission for Tourism spice trade data; WWF Tanzania fishing statistics;
Tanzanian meat: goat/sheep most widely consumed, beef and chicken significant, pork low (majority Muslim);
Staples: ugali (maize ~100 kg/cap/yr equivalent), rice, cassava, beans all high; sweet potato significant;
Fish: Nile perch (Lake Victoria massive), dagaa silver cyprinid, tilapia high; Indian Ocean coastal fish;
Zanzibar: cloves (90% of production from Zanzibar once world leader), coconut, vanilla, nutmeg major spices.
