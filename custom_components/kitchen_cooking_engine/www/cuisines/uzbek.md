FAO Fisheries Uzbekistan profile (doubly landlocked, minimal fish);
Protein: lamb/mutton #1 cultural meat; beef significant; chicken growing; pork absent (Muslim country);
Meat consumption: ~15-18 kg/cap/year total (lower than EU due to income levels);
Fat: cottonseed oil traditional but sunflower oil replacing it; lamb fat (dumba) essential in plov;
Wheat: very high per-capita ~200+ kg/cap/year; non (flatbread) daily;
Rice: very high in Fergana valley plov tradition; Devzira variety world-famous;
Fruit: Uzbekistan world's finest stone fruits and melons (GI-protected varieties multiple);
Cotton: historically dominant crop; being replaced by food crops post-independence.
