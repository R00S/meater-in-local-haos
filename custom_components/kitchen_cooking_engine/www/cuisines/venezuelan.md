## Research notes

Researched 2026-05-03. All searches conducted in Spanish first (native language), English used
only to cross-check statistics where Spanish sources gave partial data.

Sources:
- agrolatam.com / noticiasvenevision.com: "Venezuela consumo de carne 2025" — 45 kg/cápita total;
  pollo 29–30 kg (62–65%), res ~10 kg, cerdo ~5 kg.
- elsitioporcino.com / 3tres3.com: Venezuela livestock production annual report.
- primicia.com.ve / impactove.com: 65% of Venezuelan animal protein comes from poultry.
- acfiman.org / scielo.isciii.es: "Patrones de consumo de frutas y hortalizas en Venezuela" —
  tomate, cebolla, zanahoria, lechuga, auyama are most consumed vegetables.
- USDA FAS / diariorepublica.com / fedeagro.org: Maíz 1.36–1.5M t produced, 2M t consumed;
  arroz 683K t; maíz is THE cereal base (arepas, cachapas, bollos).
- edairynews.com / elimpulso.com / lavoceditalia.com: Leche 65 L/capita/yr; 88–90% of milk
  goes to cheese production; queso blanco duro is most consumed cheese nationally.
- Multiple Spanish culinary sources on sofrito venezolano, hallacas, onoto/achiote, papelón.
