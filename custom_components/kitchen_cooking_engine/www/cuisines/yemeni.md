## Research notes

Yemeni cuisine research completed. One of the Arab world's oldest and most distinctive culinary traditions. Saltah (national dish), mandi (slow-roasted lamb/chicken), fahsa (lamb broth), zurbian (spiced rice with meat), and bint al-sahn (honey bread) define the cuisine. Hawayij spice blend and hulba (fenugreek foam) are uniquely Yemeni flavour signatures.
