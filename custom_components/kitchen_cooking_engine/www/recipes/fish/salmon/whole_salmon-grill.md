# Whole Salmon × Grill — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile

Grilling whole salmon uses intense surface radiation and convection while bones and skin buffer direct moisture loss in the interior. Indirect-zone grilling is the dominant pattern for intact fish because direct high heat across the full body risks split skin and burnt tail before shoulder doneness. Cavity stuffing with citrus, herbs, and alliums is common and helps internal aromatic steaming during the cook. Whole-fish grilling usually targets a medium to medium-well center with moderate carryover as the backbone area releases stored heat after removal.

## Styckesprofil

Grillning av hel lax kombinerar stark strålningsvärme med konvektion samtidigt som ben och skinn skyddar insidan mot snabb uttorkning. Indirekt värme är vanligast för intakt fisk eftersom direkt hög värme över hela kroppen lätt ger bränt stjärtparti innan skulderdelen är klar. Fyllning med citrus, örter och lök används ofta och ger aromatisk ångning i bukhålan under tillagning. Hel laxfisk på grill siktar oftast mot medium till medium-well i centrum med måttlig eftervärme kring ryggradsområdet.

## Source recipes

### 1. Grilled Whole Salmon for a Crowd (North Wild Kitchen)
**Inspired by**: North Wild Kitchen — https://northwildkitchen.com/grilled-whole-salmon/  
**Serves**: 16 · **Prep**: fish stuffing + scoring · **Cook**: ~1 h · **Rest**: short hold for service

**Ingredients (core fish setup)**
- 4–5 kg whole salmon
- Salt, pepper
- Shallots, lemons, butter, dill, parsley

**Method**
1. Preheat grill for indirect cooking at **190°C / 375°F**.
2. Season cavity; fill with shallot, lemon, butter, dill, and parsley.
3. Score top skin, add lemon slices and melted butter.
4. Grill with lid closed about 1 hour.
5. **Pull at 60°C / 140°F** in the fish interior.

### 2. Grilled Whole Salmon with Lemon and Shallots (Hey Grill Hey)
**Inspired by**: Hey Grill Hey — https://heygrillhey.com/grilled-whole-salmon-lemon-shallots/  
**Serves**: whole fish format (~6 lb) · **Prep**: 5–10 min prep · **Cook**: ~45 min total variable · **Rest**: 5 min

**Ingredients (core fish setup)**
- 1 cleaned whole salmon (~6 lb)
- Shallots, lemons, dill fronds
- Melted butter, kosher salt, black pepper

**Method**
1. Preheat grill to **400–425°F** over indirect heat.
2. Season cavity and stuff with shallot, lemon, dill.
3. Score top of fish, butter and season exterior.
4. Roast on grill with lid closed; continue until internal target is reached.
5. **Pull at 140°F** internal temperature.
6. Rest briefly before serving from whole fish.

### 3. How to Grill a Whole Salmon (A License To Grill)
**Inspired by**: A License To Grill — https://alicensetogrill.com/how-to-grill-a-whole-salmon/  
**Serves**: whole fish (3–5 lb) · **Prep**: optional brine + stuffing · **Cook**: 20–30 min (thickness-dependent) · **Rest**: short hold before deboning/service

**Ingredients (core fish setup)**
- Whole cleaned salmon (3–5 lb)
- Olive oil
- Lemon, lime, onion, garlic, herbs
- Salt and pepper

**Method**
1. Preheat grill to **400°F**.
2. Pat fish dry, oil and season inside/outside.
3. Stuff cavity with citrus, onion, garlic, and herbs.
4. Grill with lid closed until center reaches target.
5. Site guidance: preferred pull around **130°F**, with note that FDA guidance cites **145°F**.
6. Serve after skin/spine separation.
