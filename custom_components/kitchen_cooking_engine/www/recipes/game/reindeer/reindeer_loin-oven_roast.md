# Reindeer Loin × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile

Reindeer loin (*renfilé*) can be oven-roasted whole (typically 300–700 g per piece) using the low-temperature Scandinavian method: sear in a hot pan to develop colour, then finish in an oven at 100–125°C / 212–257°F with a probe inserted until the target temperature is reached. The low oven temperature prevents carryover from overshooting target doneness in the lean, heat-sensitive fillet. The loin, like the roast cuts, benefits from juniper berries, lingonberries, or cloudberries as accompaniments — classic Scandinavian flavour pairings for reindeer.
