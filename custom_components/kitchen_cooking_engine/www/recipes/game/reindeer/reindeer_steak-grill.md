## Cut profile

Reindeer (*Rangifer tarandus*) is the traditional game meat of Sápmi (Lapland), northern Finland, Norway, and Sweden. The fillet (renfilé) and backstrap (sadel) are the premium cuts for pan searing. Reindeer meat is very lean, deep red, and has a delicate sweet-earthy flavour milder than moose. Because of its leanness it must be treated like venison: high heat, short cooking time, pulled well below well-done. Medium-rare (57 °C) is the Nordic standard.

Target internal temperature: **55–57 °C (131–135 °F)** for medium (Nordic preference). Pull at 52 °C.

## Source recipes

### 1. Köket.se temperature guide — Grillad renfilé (Swedish grilling tradition)
**Source**: Köket.se; https://www.koket.se/innertemperatur-viltkott (fetched 2026-04-24)
*Temperature guidance for grilled reindeer: Renfilé medium-rare 52 °C, medium 57 °C.*

**Ingredients** (standard Swedish preparation)
- 2 reindeer loin steaks (renfilé/renryggsfilé), approx. 150 g each
- 2 tbsp rapeseed oil
- 1 tsp coarse salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 2 juniper berries, crushed (optional)

**Method**
1. Brush steaks with oil and season with salt and pepper 15 minutes before grilling.
2. Preheat grill to high. Grill steaks 2–3 minutes per side over direct heat.
3. Insert probe. **Pull at 52 °C (126 °F)** for medium-rare.
4. Rest 5 minutes. Serve with lingonberry sauce and grilled vegetables.
*Final resting temperature: 55–57 °C (131–135 °F).*

### 2. Madensverden.dk — Grillet rensdyr (Danish/Nordic grilling)
**Source**: Madensverden.dk; https://madensverden.dk/rensdyr-opskrifter/ (web search 2026-04-24)
**Serves**: 2 · **Prep**: 1 hr marinade · **Cook**: 8 min

**Ingredients**
- 2 reindeer backstrap steaks (approx. 180 g each)
- 3 tbsp rapeseed oil
- 1 tbsp fresh rosemary
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- Salt and pepper
- Cloudberry jam (multebær syltetøj) to serve

**Method**
1. Mix oil, rosemary, and garlic. Marinate steaks 1 hour.
2. Hot grill. Sear 2–3 min per side.
3. Insert probe. **Pull at 52–54 °C (126–129 °F)** for medium-rare.
4. Rest 4 minutes. Serve with cloudberry jam and grilled root vegetables.
*Final resting temperature: 55–57 °C (131–135 °F).*

### 3. Kotikokki.net — Poropihvi grillissä (Finnish grilled reindeer steak)
**Source**: Kotikokki.net; https://www.kotikokki.net/reseptit/haku/?hakusana=poropihvi+grilli (web search 2026-04-24)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 30 min · **Cook**: 10 min

*Simple Finnish preparation — butter-brushed and grilled with minimal seasoning.*

**Ingredients**
- 4 reindeer steaks, approx. 150 g each
- 2 tbsp melted butter
- Coarse salt
- Freshly ground black pepper
- Lingonberry compote to serve

**Method**
1. Brush steaks with melted butter. Season with salt and pepper.
2. Hot grill or cast-iron grill pan. Cook 2–3 minutes each side.
3. Insert probe. **Pull at 52–54 °C (126–129 °F)**.
4. Rest 5 minutes. Serve with lingonberry compote, roasted roots, and Finnish rye bread.
*Final resting temperature: 55–57 °C (131–135 °F).*
