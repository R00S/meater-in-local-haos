# Pork Loin Steak × Pan Fry — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile

Pork loin steak (koteletsteg, Lendensteak vom Schwein) is a boneless steak cut from the loin (between chop and tenderloin area). It is lean and uniform in thickness, making it ideal for pan frying with the same technique as pork chop: hot pan, butter baste, 3–4 min per side. It is slightly more forgiving than tenderloin but dries out faster than a shoulder steak. Target core: 63–65 °C.

Target internal temperatures (pan fry):
- Juicy/pink: **63–65 °C (145–149 °F)**

## Source recipes

### 1. Madens Verden — Pandestegning af koteletter (adapted for loin steak) (Danish tradition)
**Source**: Holger Rørby Madsen, Madensverden.dk; https://madensverden.dk/pandestegning-af-koteletter/ (fetched 2026-04-24). *Classic Danish pan-fry technique applied to loin steak: salt 20 min ahead, butter baste 4 min per side in two phases.*
**Serves**: 2 · **Prep**: 20 min salting · **Cook**: 8 min

**Temperature guidance**: 2 min high heat + 2 min reduced heat per side = 4 min total per side. Core 62–65 °C.

**Ingredients**
- 2 pork loin steaks, 150–200 g each
- Generous butter knob
- 2 garlic cloves, rosemary, thyme
- Salt (20 min ahead), pepper

**Method**
1. Salt steaks 20 min before. Pat dry.
2. Heat pan to high; add butter; let foam.
3. Sear **2 min per side** on high without moving.
4. Reduce heat; add garlic and herbs. Baste continuously for **2 more min per side**.
5. Season with pepper. Insert MEATER probe. **Pull at 62–63 °C (144–145 °F)**.
6. Rest 5 minutes. Serve with potatoes and a pan sauce.
*Final temperature: 63–65 °C (145–149 °F).*

### 2. ichkoche.at — Schweinskotelett Grundrezept (adapted for boneless loin steak) (Austrian tradition)
**Source**: ichkoche.at; https://www.ichkoche.at/schweinskotelett-rezept-236782 (fetched 2026-04-24). *Austrian fundamentals: very hot dry pan then oil, 2 min each side; butter + herbs + garlic baste between sides.*
**Serves**: 2 · **Cook**: 5 min

**Temperature guidance**: 2 min per side undisturbed; butter-baste.

**Ingredients**
- 2 pork loin steaks
- Neutral oil
- 1–2 garlic cloves, 1 rosemary sprig, 3–4 thyme sprigs
- 1 tbsp butter
- Salt and pepper

**Method**
1. Heat pan dry until very hot. Add oil.
2. Sear loin steaks **2 min undisturbed** on first side.
3. Turn; add garlic, herbs, butter. Baste **2 more minutes**.
4. Remove; season; serve immediately.
5. Insert MEATER probe. **Core: 63–65 °C (145–149 °F)**.
*Final temperature: 63–65 °C (145–149 °F).*

### 3. Directo al Paladar — Chuleta de cerdo a la plancha (Spanish tradition, adapted for boneless loin steak)
**Source**: Directo al Paladar; https://www.directoalpaladar.com/recetas-de-carnes-y-aves/como-hacer-mejores-chuletas-cerdo-a-plancha-jugosas-churruscaditas (fetched 2026-04-24). *The Spanish griddle method for pork: atemperar 15 min, medium-high heat, 1.5 min per side, salt after with flakes, rest 1 min.*
**Serves**: 1–2 · **Prep**: 15 min · **Cook**: 3 min

**Temperature guidance**: 1.5 min per side on medium-high. "Respect the temperature of the meat and pan." Core: 63–65 °C.

**Ingredients**
- 2 pork loin steaks
- A few drops olive oil (on the meat)
- Flaked salt, black pepper (after)

**Method**
1. Remove steaks from fridge 15 min ahead. Rub with drops of olive oil.
2. Heat griddle pan to medium-high.
3. Cook **1.5 minutes per side** undisturbed.
4. Season with flaked salt and pepper. Rest 1 min. Serve.
5. Insert MEATER probe. **Core: 63–65 °C (145–149 °F)**.
*Final temperature: 63–65 °C (145–149 °F).*
