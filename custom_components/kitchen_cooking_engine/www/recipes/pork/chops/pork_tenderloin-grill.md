# Pork Tenderloin × Grill — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile

Pork tenderloin (svinemørbrad / Schweinefilet / solomillo de cerdo) is the leanest cut of pork and the most forgiving for pan searing — it cooks quickly (whole tenderloin: 12–15 min total; medallions: 2 min per side) and is best served slightly pink at 63–65 °C. Remove silverskin before cooking. Two main approaches: (1) slice into medallions and sear each side 2 minutes — fastest; (2) sear the whole tenderloin on all sides then finish in the oven at 200 °C × 15 min.

Target internal temperatures (grill):
- Juicy/slightly pink: **63–65 °C (145–149 °F)**

## Source recipes

### 1. Madens Verden — Svinemørbrad på grill (Danish tradition)
**Source**: Holger Rørby Madsen, Madensverden.dk; https://madensverden.dk/grill-svinemoerbrad-paa-gasgrill-weber-q3200/ (fetched 2026-04-24). *Gas or charcoal grill: brown all sides 1 min each at high, then medium direct heat 2 min per quarter-turn, basting with BBQ sauce. Total 12 min. Core 65 °C. Rest 10–15 min uncovered.*
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 12 min grill + 10 min rest

*Translation: Pork tenderloin on the gas or kettle grill. "Core temperature of the grilled pork tenderloin should be 65 °C — then it is perfectly juicy."*

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**: "Brug et stegetermometer, og en svinemørbrad på grillen skal have en kernetemperatur på 65 grader." (Use a meat thermometer; core must reach 65 °C.) At 62 °C: slightly more pink. At 68–70 °C: well done.

**Ingredients**
- 1 pork tenderloin (silverskin removed)
- 0.5 dl BBQ sauce / grill sauce (homemade or bought)
- Salt and freshly ground pepper

**Method**
1. Remove silverskin. No marinating needed for this recipe.
2. Heat gas or charcoal grill to high. Direct heat throughout.
3. Place tenderloin. Brown **1 minute per side** on all 4 sides at high heat.
4. Reduce heat to medium. Continue cooking **2 minutes per quarter-turn**, brushing with BBQ sauce each turn. Repeat 4 turns.
5. Total grill time: approximately **12 minutes** including browning.
6. Insert MEATER probe. **Pull at 63–65 °C (145–149 °F)**.
7. Rest on a chopping board **10–15 minutes** uncovered — do not cover.
8. Slice diagonally; serve with grilled potatoes and coleslaw.
*Final resting temperature: 65–68 °C (149–154 °F).*

### 2. Chefkoch.de — Mariniertes ganzes Schweinefilet vom Grill (German tradition)
**Source**: Mick1967, Chefkoch.de; https://www.chefkoch.de/rezepte/2817351433710140/Mariniertes-ganzes-Schweinefilet-vom-Grill.html (fetched 2026-04-24). *Overnight ketchup, oil, and herb marinade; quick sear on all sides over direct heat, then 25–30 min indirect at 180 °C with lid. 4.6/5, 35 ratings.*
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: overnight marinade · **Cook**: 30 min grill

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**: "In the middle of the grill rack at closed lid and approx. 180 °C (lid thermometer) for 25–30 minutes indirect." Core target: 63–65 °C.

**Ingredients**
- 2 pork tenderloins
- 1 tbsp sweet paprika powder
- 1 tbsp dried basil
- 1 tbsp dried thyme
- 1 tbsp dried rosemary
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
- 1 tbsp water
- 100 ml neutral oil

**Method**
1. Mix ketchup and water. Mix dried spices in a cup; add ketchup-water; stir; slowly whisk in oil.
2. Place tenderloins on cling film; coat all sides with marinade and massage in. Cover; refrigerate **overnight**.
3. Set up 2-zone fire (direct + indirect) in kettle or gas grill.
4. Over direct heat, sear tenderloins on all sides briefly — about 1 min per side.
5. Move to indirect zone in the **centre of the grill rack**. Close lid. Grill at **180 °C for 25–30 minutes**.
6. Insert MEATER probe before indirect phase. **Pull at 63–65 °C (145–149 °F)**.
7. Rest briefly; slice and serve.
*Final temperature: 65–68 °C (149–154 °F).*

### 3. Directo al Paladar — Solomillo de cerdo a la plancha (adapted for grill) (Spanish tradition)
**Source**: Directo al Paladar; https://www.directoalpaladar.com/recetas-de-carnes-y-aves/solomillo-cerdo-a-plancha-receta-ideal-para-cena-rapida-lista-15-minutos (fetched 2026-04-24). *The medallion method adapted for a grill: slice into 5 cm medallions, grill 2 min per side on medium-high direct heat. Fast and simple.*
**Serves**: 2 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 4 min

**Temperature guidance (from recipe adapted)**: 2 minutes per side for medallions. No marinating needed; season after.

**Ingredients**
- 1 pork tenderloin, sliced into 5 cm medallions
- Olive oil spray or light brush
- Flaked salt (after cooking)
- Ground black pepper

**Method**
1. Slice tenderloin into 4–5 cm medallions. Brush lightly with oil.
2. Heat grill to medium-high.
3. Cook medallions **2 minutes per side** without moving.
4. Remove. Season with flaked salt and pepper.
5. Insert MEATER probe. **Core: 63–65 °C (145–149 °F)**.
6. Serve with grilled vegetables.
*Final temperature: 63–65 °C (145–149 °F).*
