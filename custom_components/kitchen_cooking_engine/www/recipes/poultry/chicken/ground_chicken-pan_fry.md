## Cut profile

Ground chicken cooked by pan-frying covers two related applications: formed patties (burgers, meatballs) browned in a skillet, and crumbled ground chicken cooked loose. Ground chicken is very lean and prone to drying out; binders such as egg and breadcrumbs or cooked rice help retain moisture in formed shapes. Crumbled ground chicken browns and cooks fast — typically under 10 minutes over medium-high heat.

Target internal temperature: **165 °F (74 °C)** — USDA minimum for ground poultry; visual: no pink, juices clear

## Styckesprofil

Malet kyckling tillagat genom pannstekning täcker två relaterade tillämpningar: formade biffar (burgare, köttbullar) brynnade i en stekpanna, och löst kokat malet kyckling. Malet kyckling är mycket magert och klibbigt.

Binda formade biffar med ägg och panko. Pannsteka vid medel-hög värme tills genomstekt och lätt brynnat. Innertemperatur: **74 °C (165 °F)** USDA. Eftertillagning: 2–3 °C. Sond in i tjockaste delen av biff eller formblandning. Vila 3–5 minuter.

## Source recipes

### 1. Jessica Gavin — Turkey Burgers (ground poultry patty technique adapted for chicken)
**Inspired by**: Jessica Gavin, jessicagavin.com; https://www.jessicagavin.com/turkey-burgers/ (fetched 2025-07-15).
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 15 min + 30 min chill · **Cook**: 10–12 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Temperature guidance**:
- Pan heat: medium
- Internal target: 160–165 °F (71–74 °C)
- Time: ~5 min per side for 1/2-inch patties

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb ground chicken (85–93% lean)
- 1 large egg
- 3 tbsp panko breadcrumbs
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped (optional)
- 1 tbsp neutral oil (canola or avocado) for frying

**Method (patties)**
1. Place ground chicken in a large bowl. Add egg, panko, soy sauce, smoked paprika, garlic, onion powder, salt, pepper, and parsley.
2. Using a fork or clean hands, mix until just combined — stop as soon as ingredients are incorporated.
3. Divide into 4 equal portions. Wet your hands under cold water to prevent sticking.
4. Form each portion into a patty approximately 4 inches wide and 1/2 inch thick. Press the centre slightly thinner than the edges (patties puff in the middle when cooking).
5. Transfer patties to a plate, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate 30 minutes to firm up. This is critical for ground chicken patties — they are stickier than beef and need chilling to hold shape.
6. Heat a cast-iron or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add oil and heat until shimmering.
7. Add patties — do not crowd. Cook in batches if needed.
8. Cook first side undisturbed 5 minutes until a golden-brown crust forms.
9. Flip gently with a thin spatula. Cook another 5 minutes.
10. Check internal temp at centre: 160–165 °F (71–74 °C).
11. Rest 3 minutes before serving on buns or over salad.

**Method (crumbled — for tacos, pasta, bowls)**
1. Season ground chicken with salt, pepper, and preferred spices.
2. Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Add ground chicken. Let it sit undisturbed 2 minutes for browning, then break into pieces.
4. Stir and break up continuously until no pink remains and any moisture has evaporated — about 7–8 min total.
5. Internal temp: 165 °F (74 °C) throughout.

### 2. Natasha's Kitchen — Chicken Meatballs (ground chicken pan-fry reference)
**Inspired by**: Natasha Kravchuk, Natasha's Kitchen; https://natashaskitchen.com/chicken-meatballs/ (fetched 2025-07-15).
**Serves**: 4–6 · **Prep**: 20 min · **Cook**: 7 min · **Rest**: 2 min

**Temperature guidance**:
- Pan heat: medium
- Internal target: 165 °F (74 °C), no pink
- Time: 4 min first side + flip + 3 min covered

**Ingredients**
- 1–1.25 lb ground chicken
- 1 cup cooked white rice, cooled
- 1 large egg
- 1 small onion, grated
- 1 garlic clove, pressed
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 2 tbsp neutral oil

**Method**
1. Combine ground chicken, cooled rice, egg, grated onion, pressed garlic, salt, and pepper.
2. Mix gently — do not overwork.
3. With wet hands, roll into 1–1.25 inch balls, or flatten into small patties.
4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat until shimmering.
5. Add meatballs or patties in a single layer without crowding.
6. Cook first side undisturbed 4 minutes until golden brown.
7. Flip gently with tongs. Cover pan with a lid.
8. Cook covered 3 minutes — the steam inside the covered pan gently finishes the interior without drying the outside.
9. Remove lid. Check internal temperature: 165 °F (74 °C), no pink visible when cut in half.
10. Rest 2 minutes before serving.

### 3. Swasthi Shreekanth — Chicken Tikki (Indian Pan-Fried Ground Chicken Patties)
**Inspired by**: Swasthi Shreekanth, IndianHealthyRecipes.com; https://www.indianhealthyrecipes.com/chicken-patties/ (fetched 2026-04-24).
**Serves**: 8 patties · **Prep**: 20 min + 2 hr optional brine · **Cook**: 10–15 min

**Temperature guidance**:
- Pan heat: medium; enough oil to coat the base (~2 tbsp)
- Internal target: **165 °F (74 °C)** — confirmed in recipe; no pink should remain in centre
- Time: ~5–6 min per side; thicker patties take longer; probe the thickest patty

**Ingredients**
- 450 g chicken breast, poached or braised (then ground/minced); *or 450 g ready ground chicken*
- 1 tsp garam masala
- ½ tsp chilli powder (or paprika for mild)
- ¼ tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp fresh ginger, finely grated
- 1–2 green chillies, finely chopped (optional)
- 2 tbsp fresh coriander (cilantro), chopped
- ½ medium onion, finely grated (squeeze out excess moisture)
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 egg, beaten (for crumb-coating)
- 60 g fine breadcrumbs (for crumb-coating)
- 2–3 tbsp oil for frying
- *Optional brine*: ¼ cup yogurt mixed with ½ cup water; soak chicken 2 hr before grinding

**Method**
1. *If using whole chicken breast*: simmer in broth or water until just cooked; drain and cool, then mince finely.
2. Combine ground chicken, garam masala, chilli powder, turmeric, ginger, green chilli, coriander, onion, and salt in a bowl; mix well until evenly seasoned.
3. Divide into 8 equal portions; press each into a round patty ~1 cm (½ in) thick.
4. Dip each patty in beaten egg, then press firmly into breadcrumbs on both sides.
5. Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed skillet over medium heat until shimmering.
6. Add patties in a single layer; cook 5–6 minutes undisturbed on the first side until golden-brown crust forms.
7. Flip carefully; cook a further 5–6 minutes until the second side is golden and the internal temperature reaches **165 °F (74 °C)**.
8. Drain briefly on paper towels; serve immediately with mint-coriander chutney or inside burger buns.

## Practical tips

### Ground chicken vs. ground turkey vs. ground beef
- Ground chicken is leaner and more delicate than ground turkey (which itself is leaner than beef).
- It has a finer, moister texture that makes it stickier when raw — this is why binders and chilling are essential.
- Because of the lack of fat, it benefits more than other ground meats from added fat sources: egg yolk, a small amount of oil in the mixture, or grated onion.

### The covered-flip technique for meatballs
- Covering after flipping is a classic technique (used widely in Eastern European cooking) for round or thick shapes.
- The trapped steam cooks the top hemisphere without drying the bottom hemisphere.
- If the interior is not fully cooked at the end of the 3-minute covered phase, add 1–2 more minutes covered on low heat.

### Crumbled ground chicken (for tacos, pasta, bowls)
- Cook over medium-high to drive off moisture quickly — if the pan temperature is too low, the chicken steams rather than browns.
- Do not stir continuously — let it sit 2 minutes to develop browning before breaking up.
- Season after browning (not before) — salt draws out moisture and inhibits browning if added too early.

### Freezing formed patties
- Form patties, place parchment paper between layers, freeze in a zip-lock bag up to 3 months.
- Cook from frozen: add 4–5 extra minutes per side at medium-low heat with a lid.
- Check internal temp in the centre; do not rely on colour alone for frozen-then-cooked poultry.

### Applications for pan-fried ground chicken
| Application | Notes |
|-------------|-------|
| Tacos | Season with cumin, chili, garlic; cook crumbled |
| Pasta Bolognese | Brown crumbled, add tomato sauce, simmer 20 min |
| Lettuce wraps | Asian seasoning, serve in butter lettuce cups |
| Rice bowls | Korean-style with gochujang, sesame, soy |
| Stuffed peppers | Browned crumbled + rice + sauce, baked 25 min |

### MEATER applicability
- Ground chicken cooked crumbled cannot be probed reliably.
- For formed patties 3/4 inch or thicker, a MEATER probe inserted through the side into the centre works.
- Always confirm with visual check (no pink) + spot thermometer.
