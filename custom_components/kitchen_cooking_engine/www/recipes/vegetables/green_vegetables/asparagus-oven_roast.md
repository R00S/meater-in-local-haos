# Asparagus × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Asparagus (*Asparagus officinalis*) oven-roasted at 200–220 °C achieves golden-crisp tips and tender stems in 12–18 minutes. The dry oven heat concentrates flavour and caramelises the cut tips and natural sugars. Thin spears (≤ 6 mm) need 10–12 minutes; medium (8–12 mm) need 14–16 minutes; thick (≥ 15 mm) need 16–20 minutes. Pull temperature: 82–86 °C (180–187 °F) for crispy-tipped, tender-stemmed asparagus. MEATER probe inserted along the long axis of the thickest spear from the cut base end.

## Styckesprofil

Sparris (*Asparagus officinalis*) ugnsrostad vid 200–220 °C uppnår gyllenknapriga toppar och möra stjälkar på 12–18 minuter. Den torra ugns-värmen koncentrerar smaken och karameliserar de naturliga sockerarterna. Olja generöst, rosta på ugnsplåt i ett lager.

## Source recipes

### 1. Asparagi al Forno (inspired by Marcella Hazan)
**Inspired by**: Marcella Hazan, *Essentials of Classic Italian Cooking* (Alfred A. Knopf, 1992)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 15–18 min · **Rest**: 2 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g thick asparagus, woody ends snapped off
- 4 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- ½ tsp fine sea salt
- 30 g Parmigiano-Reggiano, freshly grated
- Freshly ground black pepper

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 220 °C. Lay asparagus in a single layer on a large sheet pan. Drizzle with olive oil; roll to coat. Season with salt.
2. Insert MEATER probe along axis of the thickest spear from cut end, tip at mid-point.
3. Roast 13–16 minutes, shaking once, until tips are golden-crisp.
4. **Pull at 82–86 °C (180–187 °F).** Scatter Parmesan and black pepper immediately (the heat melts the cheese). Final serving temperature: **82–86 °C / 180–187 °F** (golden-crisp, Parmesan-crusted).

### 2. Oven-Roasted Asparagus with Cumin (inspired by Madhur Jaffrey)
**Inspired by**: Madhur Jaffrey, *At Home with Madhur Jaffrey* (Alfred A. Knopf, 2010)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 15–18 min · **Rest**: 2 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g asparagus, woody ends removed
- 2 tbsp sunflower oil
- 1 tsp whole cumin seeds
- ½ tsp turmeric
- ½ tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
- ½ tsp fine salt
- Lemon juice and fresh coriander, to serve

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 220 °C. Toss asparagus with oil, cumin, turmeric, chilli and salt. Spread on sheet pan.
2. Insert MEATER probe along axis of thickest spear.
3. Roast 15–18 minutes until golden-crisp at tips.
4. **Pull at 82–86 °C (180–187 °F).** Squeeze lemon; scatter coriander. Final serving temperature: **82–86 °C / 180–187 °F** (spiced, golden, fragrant).

---

### 3. Roasted Asparagus with Tahini and Lemon (inspired by Yotam Ottolenghi)
**Inspired by**: Yotam Ottolenghi, *Plenty More* (Ebury Press, 2014)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 15–18 min · **Rest**: 2 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g thick asparagus, woody ends removed
- 3 tbsp olive oil; salt and pepper
- *Tahini:* 3 tbsp tahini; 2 tbsp lemon juice; 1 garlic clove grated; 2–3 tbsp water; salt
- 30 g pomegranate seeds; 10 g mint

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 220 °C. Toss asparagus with oil, salt, pepper. Spread on sheet pan.
2. Insert MEATER probe along axis of thickest spear.
3. Roast 15–18 minutes until golden-crisp.
4. **Pull at 82–86 °C (180–187 °F).** Drizzle tahini; scatter pomegranate seeds and mint. Final serving temperature: **82–86 °C / 180–187 °F** (golden, tahini-creamy, jewel-bright).

---

### 4. Espárragos Blancos al Horno (inspired by José Andrés)
**Inspired by**: José Andrés & Richard Wolffe, *Vegetables Unleashed* (Clarkson Potter, 2019)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 15–18 min · **Rest**: 2 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g white or green asparagus, woody ends peeled and trimmed
- 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- Flor de sal
- 1 tbsp sherry vinegar
- 30 g Manchego, finely shaved

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 210 °C. Drizzle asparagus with oil; season with salt. Lay on sheet pan.
2. Insert MEATER probe along axis of thickest spear.
3. Roast 15–18 minutes.
4. **Pull at 82–86 °C (180–187 °F).** Drizzle sherry vinegar; scatter Manchego shavings. Final serving temperature: **82–86 °C / 180–187 °F** (golden, Spanish — elegant classic).
