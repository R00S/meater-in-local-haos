# Broccoli × Air Fryer — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Broccoli (*Brassica oleracea* var. *italica*) in the air fryer becomes golden-crisp at the floret tips and tender at the stems in 10–14 minutes at 190–200 °C — faster and crispier than a conventional oven. The air fryer's rapid convection dehydrates the floret surface quickly, enabling Maillard browning before the interior overcooks. Florets should be 3–5 cm, similar in size for even cooking, and patted completely dry. Pull temperature: 80–85 °C (176–185 °F) for crispy-golden, tender-stemmed florets. MEATER probe inserted through the stem of the largest floret.

## Styckesprofil

Broccoli (*Brassica oleracea* var. *italica*) i airfryern blir gyllenkrispa vid blomtopparna och mjuk vid stjälkarna på 10–14 minuter vid 190–200 °C — snabbare och krispigare än ugn. Dela i buketter, olja, krydda. Klar när topparna är gyllenbryna och krispa.

## Source recipes

### 1. Madhur Jaffrey — Broccoli Tikka in the Air Fryer (Indian tradition)
**Source**: Madhur Jaffrey, *Curry Easy* (Ebury Press, 2010); tikka technique applied to broccoli florets, adapted for air fryer.
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min + 20 min marinating · **Cook**: 12–14 min · **Rest**: 2 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g broccoli, cut into 4 cm florets
- *Tikka marinade:* 100 g full-fat yoghurt; 2 tbsp sunflower oil; 2 garlic cloves (minced); 1 tsp grated ginger; 1 tsp Kashmiri chilli; 1 tsp garam masala; 1 tsp ground cumin; ½ tsp turmeric; ½ tsp fine salt; juice of ½ lemon
- Lemon wedges, onion rings, fresh coriander and mint chutney, to serve

**Method**
1. Toss florets in marinade; marinate 20 minutes. Place in a single layer in air fryer basket.
2. Insert MEATER probe through the stem of the largest floret.
3. Air fry at **200 °C for 12–14 min**, shaking once at 7 minutes.
4. **Pull at 82–85 °C (180–185 °F).** Serve with lemon, coriander, onion rings and mint chutney. Final serving temperature: **82–85 °C / 180–185 °F** (charred, spiced, juicy — Indian tandoor character).

### 2. Fuchsia Dunlop — Air-Fried Broccoli with Oyster Sauce (Chinese tradition)
**Source**: Fuchsia Dunlop, *Every Grain of Rice: Simple Chinese Home Cooking* (W. W. Norton, 2012); Chinese broccoli stir-fry technique adapted for air fryer.
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 12–14 min · **Rest**: 2 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g broccoli florets
- 2 tbsp neutral oil
- *Sauce:* 2 tbsp oyster sauce; 1 tbsp light soy sauce; 1 tbsp sesame oil; 1 tsp caster sugar; 1 garlic clove grated
- 1 tsp toasted sesame seeds; spring onion, to finish

**Method**
1. Toss florets with neutral oil. Air fry at **200 °C for 10 min**, shaking once.
2. Insert MEATER probe through largest floret stem.
3. Remove; toss in sauce; return at 190 °C for 2–4 more minutes until sauce caramelises.
4. **Pull at 82–85 °C (180–185 °F).** Scatter sesame seeds and spring onion. Final serving temperature: **82–85 °C / 180–185 °F** (golden, oyster-sauce-glossed).

---

### 3. Maangchi — Korean Spicy Air-Fried Broccoli (Korean tradition)
**Source**: Maangchi (Emily Kim), *Maangchi's Big Book of Korean Cooking* (Houghton Mifflin Harcourt, 2019); Korean spicy vegetable technique.
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 12–14 min · **Rest**: 2 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g broccoli florets; 2 tbsp oil
- *Sauce:* 2 tbsp gochujang; 1 tbsp soy sauce; 1 tbsp honey; 1 tbsp sesame oil; 1 garlic clove minced; 1 tsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp toasted sesame seeds; spring onion, to finish

**Method**
1. Toss florets with oil. Air fry at **200 °C for 10 min**, shaking once. Insert MEATER probe.
2. Toss in sauce; return at 190 °C for 2–4 min.
3. **Pull at 82–85 °C (180–185 °F).** Scatter sesame seeds and spring onion. Final serving temperature: **82–85 °C / 180–185 °F** (spicy-sweet, gochujang-glazed).

---

### 4. Yotam Ottolenghi — Air-Fried Broccoli with Za'atar and Yoghurt (Middle Eastern / Israeli tradition)
**Source**: Yotam Ottolenghi, *Plenty* (Ebury Press, 2010); adapted for air fryer.
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 12–14 min · **Rest**: 2 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g broccoli florets; 3 tbsp olive oil; salt
- *Yoghurt:* 150 g Greek yoghurt; 1 garlic clove grated; lemon juice; salt
- 1 tbsp za'atar; 30 g pomegranate seeds; 10 g flat-leaf parsley

**Method**
1. Toss florets with olive oil and salt. Insert MEATER probe through largest floret stem.
2. Air fry at **200 °C for 12–14 min**, shaking once.
3. **Pull at 82–85 °C (180–185 °F).** Spread yoghurt on platter; pile florets over; dust za'atar; scatter pomegranate and parsley. Final serving temperature: **82–85 °C / 180–185 °F** (golden, spiced, yoghurt-cool).
