# Brussels Sprouts × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Brussels sprouts (*Brassica oleracea* var. *gemmifera*) oven-roasted at 200–220 °C become charred, golden, and sweet in 22–28 minutes. Halved cut-face-down on a sheet pan maximises the Maillard browning surface. The key: very hot oven, dry sprouts, single layer with space between them. Pull temperature: 84–88 °C (183–190 °F) for charred outer leaves with a sweet, fully tender interior.

## Styckesprofil

Brysselkål (*Brassica oleracea* var. *gemmifera*) ugnsrostad vid 200–220 °C blir bränd, gyllenbrun och söt på 22–28 minuter. Halverad snittytan ned på ugnsplåt maximerar karameliseringen. Klar när snittytan är djupt gyllenbrun och ytterbladen knapriga.

## Source recipes

### 1. Najmieh Batmanglij — Persian-Style Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Saffron and Dried Barberries
**Inspired by**: Najmieh Batmanglij, *Food of Life* (Mage Publishers, 2011); Persian spice technique applied to Brussels sprouts roasting.
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 25–28 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved; 3 tbsp vegetable oil; ½ tsp saffron dissolved in 2 tbsp warm water; ½ tsp turmeric; ½ tsp ground cinnamon; 1 tsp fine salt; 2 tbsp dried barberries (zereshk) — soak 5 min; 30 g pistachios, slivered; 15 g flat-leaf parsley

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 220 °C. Mix oil, saffron water, turmeric, cinnamon and salt. Toss sprouts in mix.
2. Place cut-face-down on a foil-lined sheet pan. Insert MEATER probe through outer leaves of largest sprout.
3. Roast 22–25 min until cut faces are deeply golden.
4. **Pull at 84–88 °C (183–190 °F).** Scatter barberries, pistachios and parsley. Final serving: **84–88 °C / 183–190 °F** (golden, saffron-fragrant, sweet-sour Persian garnish).

### 2. Magnus Nilsson — Ugnsrostad Brysselkål (Nordic / Swedish tradition)
**Inspired by**: Magnus Nilsson, *The Nordic Cookbook* (Phaidon, 2015)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 25–28 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved; 3 tbsp cold-pressed rapeseed oil; 1 tsp flaked sea salt; freshly ground white pepper; 40 g unsalted butter (to brown over heat — beurre noisette style); 1 tbsp chives, chopped

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 210 °C. Toss sprouts with rapeseed oil, salt and pepper; place cut-face-down on sheet pan. Insert MEATER probe.
2. Roast 25–28 min until deeply golden.
3. **Pull at 84–88 °C (183–190 °F).** Make brown butter in a small pan; drizzle over; scatter chives. Final serving: **84–88 °C / 183–190 °F** (golden, nutty-butter, Nordic simplicity).

---

### 3. Madhur Jaffrey — Roasted Masala Brussels Sprouts (Indian tradition)
**Inspired by**: Madhur Jaffrey, *Curry Easy* (Ebury Press, 2010)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 22–25 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g Brussels sprouts, halved; 2 tbsp oil; 1 tsp cumin; ½ tsp turmeric; ½ tsp chilli; ½ tsp salt; lemon juice; fresh coriander

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 220 °C. Toss sprouts with oil and spices; place cut-face-down on sheet pan. Insert MEATER probe.
2. Roast 22–25 min.
3. **Pull at 84–88 °C (183–190 °F).** Squeeze lemon; scatter coriander. Final serving: **84–88 °C / 183–190 °F** (spiced, golden, charred).

---

### 4. José Andrés — Coles de Bruselas al Horno con Romesco (Spanish tradition)
**Inspired by**: José Andrés & Richard Wolffe, *Vegetables Unleashed* (Clarkson Potter, 2019)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 22–25 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g Brussels sprouts, halved; 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil; flor de sal; black pepper
- *Romesco:* 60 g roasted red peppers; 30 g toasted almonds; 1 garlic clove; 1 tbsp sherry vinegar; 1 tbsp smoked paprika; 2 tbsp olive oil; salt

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 220 °C. Toss sprouts with oil; season; place cut-face-down. Insert MEATER probe.
2. Roast 22–25 min until charred-golden.
3. **Pull at 84–88 °C (183–190 °F).** Blend romesco; serve alongside. Final serving: **84–88 °C / 183–190 °F** (charred, smoky-nutty romesco, Spanish).
