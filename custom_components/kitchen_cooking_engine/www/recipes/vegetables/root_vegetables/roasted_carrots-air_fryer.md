# Roasted Carrots × Air Fryer — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Carrots (*Daucus carota*) in the air fryer become caramelised, golden, and sweet in 15–20 minutes at 190–200 °C — faster than oven-roasting. Cut into coins, batons or long halves, they concentrate their natural sugars through evaporation and develop Maillard browning on the flat cut faces. Pull temperature: 86–92 °C (187–198 °F) for tender, caramelised carrots. MEATER probe inserted along the long axis of the thickest batons.

## Styckesprofil

Morötter (*Daucus carota*) i airfryern blir karameliserade, gyllenbrun och söta på 15–20 minuter vid 190–200 °C — snabbare än ugnsstekning. Skiv i bitar, olja, krydda. Klar när gyllenbrun med karameliserade kanter och genomstekt.

## Source recipes

### 1. Madhur Jaffrey — Air-Fried Masala Carrots (Indian tradition)
**Inspired by**: Madhur Jaffrey, *Curry Easy* (Ebury Press, 2010); spice-roasted carrot adapted for air fryer.
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 16–18 min · **Rest**: 2 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g carrots, peeled and cut into 1 cm batons; 2 tbsp sunflower oil; 1 tsp cumin seeds; ½ tsp turmeric; ½ tsp Kashmiri chilli; ½ tsp ground coriander; ½ tsp fine salt; lemon juice; fresh coriander

**Method**
1. Toss carrots with oil and spices. Place in air fryer basket. Insert MEATER probe along axis of thickest carrot baton.
2. Air fry at **200 °C for 16–18 min**, shaking once at 9 min.
3. **Pull at 86–90 °C (187–194 °F).** Squeeze lemon; scatter coriander. Final serving: **86–90 °C / 187–194 °F** (caramelised, spiced, golden).

### 2. Claudia Roden — Air-Fried Moroccan Carrots with Chermoula (Moroccan / North African tradition)
**Inspired by**: Claudia Roden, *Arabesque* (Alfred A. Knopf, 2005); chermoula technique adapted for air fryer.
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 16–18 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g carrots, halved lengthwise; *chermoula:* 2 tbsp oil; 2 garlic cloves minced; 1 tsp cumin; 1 tsp paprika; ½ tsp coriander; 2 tbsp lemon juice; 15 g coriander; 1 tsp salt

**Method**
1. Blend chermoula; toss carrots in it. Insert MEATER probe along longest carrot axis.
2. Air fry at **190 °C for 16–18 min**, shaking once.
3. **Pull at 88–92 °C (190–198 °F).** Scatter fresh coriander. Final serving: **88–92 °C / 190–198 °F** (spiced, golden, North African).

---

### 3. Yotam Ottolenghi — Air-Fried Carrots with Coriander Yoghurt (Israeli / Middle Eastern tradition)
**Inspired by**: Yotam Ottolenghi, *Plenty More* (Ebury Press, 2014); air fryer adaptation.
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 16–18 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g carrots, cut into batons; 3 tbsp olive oil; 1 tsp cumin; ½ tsp turmeric; salt; *yoghurt:* 150 g Greek yoghurt; 1 garlic clove grated; 1 tbsp lemon juice; 15 g fresh coriander, blended in; pomegranate seeds and mint, to finish

**Method**
1. Toss carrots with oil and spices. Insert MEATER probe. Air fry at **200 °C for 16–18 min**, shaking once.
2. **Pull at 88–92 °C (190–198 °F).** Spread yoghurt on platter; pile carrots; scatter pomegranate and mint. Final serving: **88–92 °C / 190–198 °F** (golden, spiced, yoghurt-cool).

---

### 4. Harumi Kurihara — Air-Fried Carrots with Sesame Glaze (Japanese tradition)
**Inspired by**: Harumi Kurihara, *Harumi's Japanese Home Cooking* (DK, 2007); sesame-glazed vegetable technique adapted for air fryer.
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 14–16 min · **Rest**: 2 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g carrots, cut into 1 cm coins; 2 tbsp sesame oil; *glaze:* 2 tbsp soy sauce; 1 tbsp mirin; 1 tsp caster sugar; 1 tsp sesame oil; sesame seeds to finish

**Method**
1. Toss carrots with sesame oil. Insert MEATER probe. Air fry at **190 °C for 12 min**, shaking once.
2. Toss in glaze; return 2–4 more minutes. **Pull at 86–90 °C (187–194 °F).** Scatter sesame seeds. Final serving: **86–90 °C / 187–194 °F** (caramelised, soy-sweet, Japanese).
