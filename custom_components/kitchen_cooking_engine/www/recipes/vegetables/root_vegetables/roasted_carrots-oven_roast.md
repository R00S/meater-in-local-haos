# Roasted Carrots × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Carrots (*Daucus carota*) oven-roasted at 200–220 °C develop intensely caramelised, golden surfaces in 25–35 minutes. The natural sugars (6–8 g/100 g — higher than most vegetables) caramelise readily at these temperatures, producing a characteristic sweetness and amber colour. Carrots can be roasted whole (small/medium), halved lengthwise, cut into batons or large coins. Pull temperature: 88–92 °C (190–198 °F) for caramelised, fully tender carrots. MEATER probe inserted along the long axis of the thickest piece.

## Styckesprofil

Morötter (*Daucus carota*) ugnsrostade vid 200–220 °C utvecklar intensivt karameliserade, gyllenbruna ytor på 25–35 minuter. De naturliga sockerarterna (6–8 g/100 g — högre än de flesta grönsaker) reagerar kraftigt med Maillard-reaktion. Klar när gyllenbrun och genomstekt.

## Source recipes

### 1. Carottes Rôties à la Marocaine (inspired by Claudia Roden)
**Inspired by**: Claudia Roden, *Arabesque: A Taste of Morocco, Turkey and Lebanon* (Alfred A. Knopf, 2005)
**Serves**: 4–6 · **Prep**: 15 min · **Cook**: 30–35 min · **Rest**: 5 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g medium carrots, peeled and halved lengthwise; *chermoula:* 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil; 2 garlic cloves (minced); 1 tsp ground cumin; 1 tsp sweet paprika; ½ tsp ground coriander; 2 tbsp lemon juice; 15 g fresh coriander (chopped); 1 tsp fine salt; pinch of cayenne; optional: 3–4 saffron threads dissolved in 1 tbsp warm water

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 210 °C. Mix chermoula; toss carrots in it; spread cut-face-down on a foil-lined sheet pan.
2. Insert MEATER probe along the long axis of the thickest half-carrot.
3. Roast 30–35 minutes until cut faces are deeply caramelised and golden.
4. **Pull at 90–94 °C (194–201 °F).** Scatter additional fresh coriander. Serve as a *meze* salad, warm or at room temperature. Final serving: **warm to room temperature** (spiced, golden, intensely sweet — classic North African meze).

### 2. Gajar Roast (inspired by Madhur Jaffrey)
**Inspired by**: Madhur Jaffrey, *Curry Easy* (Ebury Press, 2010)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 28–32 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g carrots, cut into 4 cm batons; 3 tbsp sunflower oil; 1 tsp cumin seeds; ½ tsp turmeric; 1 tsp ground coriander; ½ tsp Kashmiri chilli; 1 tsp fine salt; 1 tbsp lemon juice; 15 g fresh coriander; ½ tsp garam masala

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 210 °C. Toss carrots with oil and spices; spread on sheet pan. Insert MEATER probe along thickest baton axis.
2. Roast 28–32 min, turning once at 15 min.
3. **Pull at 88–92 °C (190–198 °F).** Dust with garam masala; squeeze lemon; scatter coriander. Final serving: **88–92 °C / 190–198 °F** (spiced, golden, fragrant).

---

### 3. Roasted Carrots with Harissa and Yoghurt (inspired by Yotam Ottolenghi)
**Inspired by**: Yotam Ottolenghi, *Plenty More* (Ebury Press, 2014)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 30–35 min · **Rest**: 5 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g carrots, halved lengthwise; 3 tbsp olive oil; 1 tbsp harissa paste; 1 tsp honey; ½ tsp cumin; salt; *yoghurt:* 150 g Greek yoghurt; 1 garlic clove grated; 1 tbsp lemon juice; salt; pomegranate seeds and fresh herbs to finish

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 210 °C. Mix oil, harissa, honey, cumin, salt; toss carrots; place cut-face-down on sheet pan.
2. Insert MEATER probe along thickest carrot axis.
3. Roast 30–35 min. **Pull at 90–94 °C (194–201 °F).**
4. Spread yoghurt on platter; pile carrots; scatter pomegranate and herbs. Final serving: **warm to room temperature** (golden, harissa-spiced, pomegranate-jewelled).

---

### 4. Carote Arrosto con Erbe e Aglio (inspired by Marcella Hazan)
**Inspired by**: Marcella Hazan, *Essentials of Classic Italian Cooking* (Alfred A. Knopf, 1992)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 28–32 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g medium carrots, halved lengthwise; 4 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil; 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced; 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves; ½ tsp fine sea salt; black pepper; 1 tbsp flat-leaf parsley, to finish; optional: 2 tbsp freshly grated Parmesan

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 200 °C. Toss carrots with 3 tbsp oil, salt and pepper; spread cut-face-down. Insert MEATER probe.
2. Roast 25 min; add garlic and thyme; return 3–5 more minutes.
3. **Pull at 88–92 °C (190–198 °F).** Scatter parsley and optional Parmesan. Final serving: **88–92 °C / 190–198 °F** (caramelised, garlic-fragrant, Italian).
