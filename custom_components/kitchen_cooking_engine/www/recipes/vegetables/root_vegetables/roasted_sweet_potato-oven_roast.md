# Roasted Sweet Potato × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Sweet potato (*Ipomoea batatas*) cut into chunks or wedges and oven-roasted at 200–220 °C caramelises in 25–35 minutes, producing crispy, golden-edged pieces with a soft, sweet interior. Unlike whole baked sweet potato, chunk-roasting maximises cut-face surface area for Maillard browning. Pull temperature: 90–95 °C (194–203 °F) at the thickest chunk. MEATER probe inserted into the thickest chunk.

## Styckesprofil

Sötpotatis (*Ipomoea batatas*) skuren i bitar eller klyftor och ugnsrostad vid 200–220 °C karameliseras på 25–35 minuter, vilket ger knapriga, gyllenbruna kanter och mjuk, söt insida. Klar när gyllenbrun och genomstekt.

## Source recipes

### 1. Yotam Ottolenghi — Roasted Sweet Potato with Tahini and Za'atar (Israeli / Middle Eastern tradition)
**Source**: Yotam Ottolenghi & Sami Tamimi, *Jerusalem* (Ebury Press, 2012)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 28–32 min · **Rest**: 5 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 4 cm chunks; 3 tbsp olive oil; 1 tsp cumin; ½ tsp allspice; ½ tsp cinnamon; 1 tsp fine salt; *tahini sauce:* 3 tbsp tahini; 2 tbsp lemon juice; 1 garlic clove grated; 2 tbsp water; 30 g pomegranate seeds; 15 g fresh coriander; chilli flakes to finish

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 220 °C. Toss sweet potato chunks with olive oil and spices; spread on sheet pan. Insert MEATER probe into the thickest chunk.
2. Roast 28–32 min until caramelised and golden, turning once at 15 min.
3. **Pull at 90–95 °C (194–203 °F).** Drizzle tahini sauce; scatter pomegranate seeds, coriander and chilli. Final serving: **90–95 °C / 194–203 °F** (caramelised, spiced, tahini-creamy, jewel-bright).

### 2. Claudia Roden — Roasted Sweet Potato with Chermoula (Moroccan / North African tradition)
**Source**: Claudia Roden, *Arabesque* (Alfred A. Knopf, 2005)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 28–32 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g sweet potatoes, cut into 4 cm chunks; *chermoula:* 3 tbsp olive oil; 2 garlic cloves minced; 1 tsp cumin; 1 tsp paprika; ½ tsp coriander; 2 tbsp lemon juice; 15 g fresh coriander; 1 tsp salt; pinch saffron in 1 tbsp warm water

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 210 °C. Toss chunks in chermoula; spread on sheet pan. Insert MEATER probe.
2. Roast 28–32 min. **Pull at 90–95 °C (194–203 °F).** Scatter fresh coriander. Final serving: **90–95 °C / 194–203 °F** (spiced, golden, North African).

---

### 3. Madhur Jaffrey — Shakarkandi ki Sabzi (Indian tradition)
**Source**: Madhur Jaffrey, *Curry Easy* (Ebury Press, 2010)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 28–32 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g sweet potatoes, cut into 3 cm chunks; 2 tbsp sunflower oil; 1 tsp cumin seeds; ½ tsp turmeric; ½ tsp Kashmiri chilli; 1 tsp ground coriander; 1 tsp fine salt; 1 tsp chaat masala; lemon juice; fresh coriander

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 210 °C. Toss chunks with oil and spices; spread on sheet pan. Insert MEATER probe.
2. Roast 28–32 min. **Pull at 90–95 °C (194–203 °F).** Dust with chaat masala; squeeze lemon; scatter coriander. Final serving: **90–95 °C / 194–203 °F** (spiced, caramelised, tangy-sweet).

---

### 4. Magnus Nilsson — Roasted Sweet Potato with Rapeseed Oil and Dill (Nordic tradition)
**Source**: Magnus Nilsson, *The Nordic Cookbook* (Phaidon, 2015); Nordic treatment of the sweet potato.
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 28–32 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g sweet potatoes, cut into long wedges; 3 tbsp cold-pressed rapeseed oil; 1 tsp flaked sea salt; white pepper; 1 tbsp fresh dill; 1 tbsp fresh chives; ½ tsp lemon juice; 60 g crème fraîche, to serve

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 200 °C. Toss wedges with rapeseed oil, salt and pepper; spread on sheet pan. Insert MEATER probe.
2. Roast 28–32 min. **Pull at 90–95 °C (194–203 °F).** Scatter dill and chives; squeeze lemon. Serve with crème fraîche alongside. Final serving: **90–95 °C / 194–203 °F** (golden, rapeseed-fragrant, Nordic dill).
