# Ninja Speedi / Crispi Compatibility Guide

## Overview

This guide explains how the Ninja Combi SFP700EU recipe builder is compatible with the Ninja Speedi (Rapid Cooker) and Ninja Crispi models. While these products have different names for their cooking modes, they share similar underlying technology and can use many of the same recipes with minor adaptations.

| **Combi-Bake** | **Steam & Bake** | Same principle - steam + baking for fluffy breads and pastries |

### Important: SmartSwitch Position

**"Rapid Cook" is NOT a mode name** - it's the SmartSwitch position:
- **SmartSwitch: RAPID COOKER** = Enables Speedi Meals functionality
- When recipes say "Rapid Cook mode", they mean: Flip SmartSwitch to RAPID COOKER position, then select SPEEDI MEALS

## Recipe Conversion Guide

### Converting Ninja Combi Recipes to Speedi

**General Rules:**
1. **Mode Names:** Replace Combi mode names with Speedi equivalents (see table above)
2. **Water Amount:** Speedi adds water directly to pot, Combi uses external tank
   - Same amounts work, just different placement
3. **Cooking Times:** Usually identical, may vary by 1-2 minutes
4. **Temperature:** Use same temperatures
5. **Tray Positions:** Speedi has 2 levels (base + crisper tray) vs Combi's 3 racks

## Water Amount Guidelines

### Ninja Combi (External Water Tank)

**By Mode:**
- **Combi-Crisp:** 0.5-2 cups in external tank
  - Vegetables: 0.5 cups
  - Fresh proteins: 1 cup
  - Frozen proteins: 2 cups
- **Combi-Bake:** 0.5-2 cups in external tank
  - Minimal steam (cookies): 0 cups
  - Moderate steam (cakes): 0.5 cups
  - Full steam (bread): 1.5-2 cups
- **Combi-Meal:** 1-1.5 cups in external tank
  - PLUS water in base layer for rice/pasta

### Ninja Speedi (In-Pot Water)

**By Mode:**
- **Steam & Crisp:** 0.5-2 cups in pot
  - Vegetables: 0.5 cups
  - Fresh proteins: 1 cup
  - Frozen proteins: 2 cups
- **Steam & Bake:** 0.5-2 cups in pot
  - Same guidelines as Combi-Bake
- **Rapid Cook:** Water for base ONLY
  - No separate steam water needed
  - Rice/pasta water handles steaming

**Key Difference:**
- Combi: Separate water tank + base layer water
- Speedi: Single water source (in pot or base layer)
