# Flank Steak × Charcoal Grill — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Charcoal grill adds intense direct heat, smoke, and char that pairs especially well
with flank's beefy flavour. The same rules apply: lean, fibrous, slice against grain.
Many cultures have a tradition of flank/bavette over charcoal — Argentine asado being
the most famous, Brazilian churrasco, and French bistro tradition.

## Source recipes

### 1. Asado Argentina — Matambre/Vacio a las Brasas
- **Source**: asadoargentina.com/matambre-brasas
- **Argentine tradition**: Parrilla with quebracho charcoal, direct high heat.
  "Vuelta y vuelta" — 3-4 min/side maximum. Rest 5 min, cut thin against grain.
- **Target**: **54-58°C** (jugoso — Argentine "juicy" = pink center)
- "La vainilla/matambre sobre la parrilla caliente es el alma del asado."

### 2. Bon Appétit — Charcoal-Grilled Flank Steak with Herb Sauce (USA)
- **Source**: bonappetit.com/recipe/charcoal-grilled-flank-steak-herb-sauce
- **Method**: Two-zone charcoal setup. Sear direct 2-3 min/side then rest off heat.
  Chimichurri or herb butter finish. Thermometer check mandatory.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 3. Cuisine Actuelle — Bavette de bœuf à la braise (France)
- **Source**: cuisineactuelle.fr/recettes/bavette-braise
- **Method**: Braises vives (live coals), grille très chaude. 2-3 min/côté.
  La bavette sur la braise est une tradition du bistrot parisien.
- **Target**: **52-55°C** (saignant) — French tradition for bavette

### 4. Churrasco Brasil — Fraldinha na grelha (Brazil)
- **Source**: churrascobrasil.com.br/fraldinha-grelha
- **Brazilian fraldinha**: Charcoal grill, direct heat. 3-4 min/side for 1–1.5cm thick.
  Cortar contra a fibra obrigatoriamente.
- **Target**: **54-58°C** (ao ponto mal passado / rosé)

### 5. Braai SA — Flank Steak on the Braai (South Africa)
- **Source**: braai.co.za/recipes/flank-steak-braai
- **South African braai**: Hardwood charcoal, direct heat. Short hot cook. Slice thin.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare. "Don't go beyond medium — braai heat is unforgiving."

## Temperature consensus

| Source | Country | Target | Style |
|--------|---------|--------|-------|
| Asado Argentina | AR | 54-58°C | jugoso |
| Bon Appétit | USA | 54-57°C | medium-rare |
| Cuisine Actuelle | FR | **52-55°C** | saignant |
| Churrasco Brasil | BR | 54-58°C | ao ponto mal passado |
| Braai SA | ZA | 54-57°C | medium-rare |

**Consensus: 54-57°C medium-rare.** French bavette tradition lower end 52-54°C.
Charcoal adds smoke/char but doesn't change the target vs gas grill.
