# Flank Steak × Grill (gas) — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Flank on a gas grill: direct high heat, short time. The grill adds char marks and
a smoky character that complements the beefy flavour. Still lean and fibrous —
same hard ceiling at 63°C. Marinating before grilling is common practice.

## Source recipes

### 1. Weber Grills — Grilled Flank Steak Recipe (USA, grill authority)
- **Source**: weber.com/us/en/recipes/beef/grilled-flank-steak
- **Method**: Gas grill 230-260°C direct. 8-10 min total, flip once halfway.
  Optional marinade (soy-citrus). Rest 5 min before slicing against grain.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. The Kitchn — How to Grill Flank Steak (USA)
- **Source**: thekitchn.com/how-to-grill-flank-steak-cooking-lessons
- **Method**: Gas grill very hot. Direct heat. 4-5 min/side. Thermometer check.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare. "Flank has no fat to forgive overcooking."

### 3. ICA Recept — Grillad flanksteak (Sweden)
- **Source**: ica.se/recept/grillad-flanksteak
- **Method**: Gasgrill eller kolgrill, hög värme direkt. 3-4 min per sida.
  Vila 5 min. Skär mot fibrerna.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)
- "Bra marinerad flankbiff är en av grillens verkliga höjdpunkter."

### 4. Carne et Fuoco — Bistecca di Fianco alla Griglia (Italy)
- **Source**: carneetfuoco.it/ricette/bistecca-fianco-griglia
- **Method**: Griglia a gas o carbone, alta temperatura. 3-4 min per lato.
  Riposo obbligatorio 5 min. Tagliare obliquamente contro le fibre.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (al sangue/rosé — Italians accept medium-rare for this cut)

### 5. Asado Argentina — Matambre a la parrilla (Argentina)
- **Source**: asadoargentina.com/matambre-parrilla
- **Note**: Argentine "matambre" is the flank/skirt area; parrilla (grill) tradition.
- **Method**: Parrilla alta temperatura, vuelta y vuelta (quick flip method), 3-4 min/lado.
- **Target**: **54-60°C** — Argentine tradition is slightly more flexible; "jugoso" = 54-57°C

## Temperature consensus

| Source | Country | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Weber Grills | USA | 54-57°C |
| The Kitchn | USA | 54-57°C |
| ICA Recept | SE | 54-57°C |
| Carne et Fuoco | IT | 54-57°C |
| Asado Argentina | AR | 54-60°C |

**Consensus: 54-57°C medium-rare.** Gas grill adds char and flavour but does not
change the target temperature for this lean fibrous cut.
