# Flank Steak × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Oven-roasting flank steak is less common than grilling but works well for stuffed
preparations (braciole, pinwheel rolls) or when grilling is not available. The same
lean-fibrous rules apply — cook hot and fast, never past medium.

## Source recipes

### 1. Food Network — Oven-Roasted Flank Steak (USA)
- **Source**: foodnetwork.com/recipes/oven-roasted-flank-steak
- **Method**: Preheat oven 230°C (450°F). Sear in cast iron, then oven 8-10 min.
  Rest 5-10 min. Slice against grain.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. The Pioneer Woman — Stuffed Flank Steak (Braciole style, USA)
- **Source**: thepioneerwoman.com/food-cooking/recipes/stuffed-flank-steak
- **Method**: Stuffed pinwheel roast 180°C (350°F), 25-30 min. Thermometer required.
  Probe in thickest part of meat (not stuffing).
- **Target**: **145°F / 63°C** for stuffed (food safety). Unstuffed: 130-135°F/54-57°C.
- Note: Stuffed preparations require higher temp for safety; unstuffed stays medium-rare.

### 3. Matprat — Ovnsstekt flankbiff med urter (Norway)
- **Source**: matprat.no/oppskrifter/kjott/ovnsstekt-flankbiff
- **Method**: Forvarmet ovn 220°C, 10-12 min etter raskt steking i panne. Hvil 5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** kjernetemperatur (medium-rare)

### 4. Delicious — Oven-roasted flank steak with chimichurri (Australia)
- **Source**: delicious.com.au/recipes/oven-roasted-flank-steak-chimichurri
- **Method**: Preheat 240°C. Sear stovetop, finish oven 8-10 min. Rest 5 min.
  Slice thinly on the bias against grain.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

### 5. Küchengötter — Flanksteak aus dem Ofen (Germany)
- **Source**: kuechengotter.de/rezepte/flanksteak-ofen
- **Method**: Pfanne scharf anbraten, dann Ofen 220°C, 8-10 Min. Kerntemperatur messen.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (rosa-rot, medium-rare)

## Temperature consensus

| Source | Country | Target | Notes |
|--------|---------|--------|-------|
| Food Network | USA | 54-57°C | Standard roast |
| Pioneer Woman | USA | 54-57°C (63°C stuffed) | Stuffed needs higher for safety |
| Matprat | NO | 54-57°C | Sear then oven |
| Delicious | AU | 54-57°C | Chimichurri finish |
| Küchengötter | DE | 54-57°C | Same approach |

**Consensus: 54-57°C medium-rare.** Oven roast for flank is typically a sear-then-oven
method. Stuffed rolls require 63°C for food safety, but plain cuts stay at medium-rare.
