# Flank Steak × Pan Fry — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Pan fry for flank differs from pan sear in that it uses moderate-high heat with a
little more oil and sometimes longer contact time. Still a lean fibrous cut — the
ceiling at 63°C (medium) applies firmly. Same grain-slicing requirement.

## Source recipes

### 1. AllRecipes — Pan-Fried Flank Steak (USA community favourite, 4.5★ 300+ reviews)
- **Source**: allrecipes.com/recipe/pan-fried-flank-steak
- **Method**: Medium-high heat, oil in pan. 4-5 min/side for 1" thick. Let rest 5 min.
  "Use a meat thermometer — flank goes from perfect to tough in minutes."
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. Taste of Home — Pan-Fried Flank Steak with Onions (USA midwest tradition)
- **Source**: tasteofhome.com/recipes/pan-fried-flank-steak
- **Method**: Medium-high cast iron. Oil. 4-5 min/side. Onion gravy optional.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare. "Do not go beyond medium — it toughens."

### 3. Cuisine Journal — Bavette poêlée, sauce vierge (France)
- **Source**: cuisinejournal.fr/bavette-poele
- **French bistro**: bavette à la poêle — high butter+oil, 2-3 min/side.
- **Target**: **52-55°C** (saignant to rosé) — French never cook bavette well done.
- "La bavette est un morceau populaire et économique, mais exige une cuisson rapide."

### 4. Matprat — Stekt flankbiff (Norway)
- **Source**: matprat.no/oppskrifter/kjott/stekt-flankbiff
- **Method**: Stekepanne på høy varme. Olje. 2-3 min per side. Hvil 5 min.
  Skjær på tvers av fibrene.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

### 5. Gutekueche — Flanksteak in der Pfanne (Germany)
- **Source**: gutekueche.at/flanksteak-pfanne-rezept
- **Method**: Pfanne sehr heiß, Öl, 2-3 Min pro Seite, ruhen lassen 5 Min.
  "Immer quer zur Faser schneiden."
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare (rosa-rot)

## Temperature consensus

| Source | Country | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| AllRecipes | USA | 54-57°C |
| Taste of Home | USA | 54-57°C |
| Cuisine Journal (bavette) | FR | **52-55°C** |
| Matprat | NO | 54-57°C |
| Gutekueche | DE/AT | 54-57°C |

**Consensus: 54-57°C medium-rare.** French tradition again prefers the lower 52-55°C end.
Pan fry yields the same target as pan sear for this cut — the method difference is minimal.

## Data applied
