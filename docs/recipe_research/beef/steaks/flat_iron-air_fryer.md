# Flat Iron Steak × Air Fryer — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Air fryer flat iron steak works well — the circulating hot air gives good results
for 1-1.5cm thick cuts. Higher temperatures recommended to mimic the Maillard
reaction. The marbling helps keep it moist.

## Source recipes

### 1. Air Fryer World — Flat Iron Steak in Air Fryer (USA)
- **Source**: airfryerworld.com/flat-iron-steak-air-fryer
- **Method**: 200°C air fryer, 4-5 min each side (flip halfway). Rest 5 min.
  Optionally preheat air fryer for better sear.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. Simply Air Fryer — Air Fryer Flat Iron Steak Recipe (USA)
- **Source**: simplyairfryer.com/flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: Preheat air fryer 205°C. Season well (crust = flavour). 9-10 min total,
  flip at 5 min. Check internal temp.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 3. Airfryer Vibes — Flat Iron Steak Air Fryer (USA/UK)
- **Source**: airfryervibes.com/flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: 200°C, 8-10 min total, flip halfway. Use meat thermometer.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

### 4. ReceptKungen — Flat iron i airfryer (Sweden)
- **Source**: receptkungen.se/flat-iron-airfryer
- **Method**: Luftfrityrugn 200°C. 8-10 min totalt, vänd halvvägs. Kötttermometer.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

### 5. Just Cook — Flat Iron in Air Fryer (UK)
- **Source**: justcook.butchery.com/flat-iron-air-fryer
- **Method**: Air fryer 200°C, 9-10 min. Flip at 5 min. Rest 3-5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare
