# Flat Iron Steak × Charcoal Grill — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Charcoal gives flat iron extra smoke flavour and intense char. The cut's marbling
handles the high heat well. Direct charcoal heat is the preferred approach.

## Source recipes

### 1. Bon Appétit — Charcoal-Grilled Flat Iron Steak (USA)
- **Source**: bonappetit.com/recipe/charcoal-grilled-flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: Hardwood charcoal, high direct heat. 4-5 min/side. Rest 5-7 min.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. BBQ Host — Flat Iron Steak on Charcoal Grill (USA)
- **Source**: bbqhost.com/flat-iron-steak-charcoal-grill
- **Method**: Charcoal direct, grill grate oiled. 4-5 min/side. Internal temp check.
  "Charcoal adds a smokiness that really elevates flat iron's natural beefiness."
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 3. Hemkök — Flat iron på kolgrill (Sweden)
- **Source**: hemkok.se/flat-iron-kolgrill
- **Method**: Kolgrill med direkt värme. 4-5 min per sida. Vila 5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

### 4. CookingForEngineers — Charcoal Flat Iron (USA/international)
- **Source**: cookingforengineers.com/charcoal-flat-iron
- **Method**: Chimney-started charcoal, direct heat. 4-5 min/side, flip once.
  Thermometer required. Rest before slicing.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 5. Grillrezepte.de — Flat Iron auf dem Holzkohlegrill (Germany)
- **Source**: grillrezepte.de/flat-iron-holzkohle
- **Method**: Holzkohle direkt, hohe Hitze. 4-5 Min pro Seite. Ruhe 5 Min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (rosa/medium-rare)
