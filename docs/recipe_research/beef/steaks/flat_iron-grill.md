# Flat Iron Steak × Grill (gas) — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Flat iron on a gas grill is an excellent combination — the marbling handles grill
heat well and you get good char marks. Slightly thicker than flank/skirt so benefits
from two-zone setup if cutting is thick (1.5cm+).

## Source recipes

### 1. Weber — Grilled Flat Iron Steak (USA)
- **Source**: weber.com/us/en/recipes/beef/grilled-flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: Gas grill 230°C direct heat. 4-5 min/side for 1.5cm thick. Rest 5 min.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. The Kitchn — How to Grill Flat Iron Steak (USA)
- **Source**: thekitchn.com/how-to-grill-flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: Gas grill preheated high. Direct heat 4-5 min/side. Thermometer check.
  "Flat iron is the hidden gem of grilling — better than sirloin at half the price."
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 3. ICA Recept — Grillad flat iron (Sweden)
- **Source**: ica.se/recept/grillad-flat-iron
- **Method**: Gasgrill 230°C direkt värme. 4-5 min per sida. Vila 5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

### 4. Marmiton — Pavé de bœuf plat grillé au gaz (France)
- **Source**: marmiton.org/recettes/pavé-boeuf-grille-gaz
- **Method**: Grill à gaz maximum. 4 min/côté. Reposer 5 min. Servir tranché.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (rosé)

### 5. Woolworths Taste — Flat Iron Steak on the Gas Grill (South Africa)
- **Source**: taste.co.za/flat-iron-steak-gas-grill
- **Method**: Gas braai high heat. 4-5 min/side. Rest 5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare
