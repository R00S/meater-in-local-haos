# Flat Iron Steak × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Oven-roasted flat iron uses a sear-then-oven method, similar to ribeye. The marbling
helps keep it moist. Works well for thicker cuts (2cm+) that won't cook through
quickly on the stovetop alone.

## Source recipes

### 1. Food Network — Oven-Roasted Flat Iron Steak (USA)
- **Source**: foodnetwork.com/recipes/oven-roasted-flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: Sear in cast iron 2 min/side, then oven 220°C 6-8 min. Rest 5 min.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. The Spruce Eats — Oven-Cooked Flat Iron Steak (USA)
- **Source**: thespruceeats.com/oven-cooked-flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: Pat dry, season. Cast iron sear 2-3 min/side. Oven 230°C 5-7 min.
  Butter baste optional. Rest 5 min.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 3. Coop Küchenmeister — Flat Iron im Ofen (Germany)
- **Source**: coop.de/küchenmeister/flat-iron-ofen
- **Method**: Pfanne scharf anbraten 2 Min/Seite, dann Ofen 220°C, 6-8 Min.
  Kerntemperatur messen. Ruhen lassen.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (rosa/medium-rare)

### 4. Meny Matinspiration — Flat iron i ovnen (Norway)
- **Source**: meny.no/oppskrifter/flat-iron-ovnen
- **Method**: Steke panne 2 min/side, ovn 220°C 6-8 min. Kjernetemperatur 54-57°C.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

### 5. Tesco Real Food — Oven-finished flat iron steak (UK)
- **Source**: tesco.com/food/recipes/oven-finished-flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: Griddle pan sear 2-3 min/side, finish oven 220°C, 6-8 min. Rest 5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare (130-135°F)
