# Flat Iron Steak × Pan Fry — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Pan frying flat iron uses a bit more oil and slightly lower sustained heat than
pure searing. Works well for thicker cuts (1.5-2cm) that need more time through.
The good marbling means the fat protects it better than leaner cuts.

## Source recipes

### 1. AllRecipes — Pan-Fried Flat Iron Steak (USA, 4.6★)
- **Source**: allrecipes.com/recipe/pan-fried-flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: Medium-high heat, oil. 4-5 min/side for 1.5cm thick. Let rest 5 min.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. Spend with Pennies — Easy Pan Fried Flat Iron Steak (USA)
- **Source**: spendwithpennies.com/pan-fried-flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: Oil, medium-high heat. 4-5 min/side. Finish with herb butter.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 3. REMA 1000 — Flatjernbiff i panne (Norway)
- **Source**: rema.no/oppskrifter/flatjernbiff-panne
- **Method**: Stekepanne med olje, middels høy varme. 4 min per side. Hvil 5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare)

### 4. El Tenedor — Filete plano en sartén (Spain)
- **Source**: eltenedor.es/recetas/filete-plano-sarten
- **Method**: Sartén con aceite medio-alto. 4-5 min/lado. Reposar 5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medio crudo / al punto)

### 5. Küchenschätze — Flat Iron Steak in der Pfanne (Germany)
- **Source**: kuechenschatze.de/flat-iron-steak-pfanne
- **Method**: Pfanne mit Öl, hohe Hitze. 3-4 Min pro Seite. Ruhen lassen 5 Min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (rosa, medium-rare)
