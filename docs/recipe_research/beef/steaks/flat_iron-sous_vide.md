# Flat Iron Steak × Sous Vide — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Sous vide works excellently for flat iron — the marbling benefits from rendered fat
similar to ribeye, but the shoulder muscles also have some connective tissue that
benefits from the longer cook time. A good middle ground: 55-57°C for 1-2 hours.

## Source recipes

### 1. Serious Eats — Sous Vide Flat Iron Steak (USA)
- **Source**: seriouseats.com/sous-vide-flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: 130-135°F / 54-57°C for 1-3 hours. Sear finish 1 min/side.
  "Flat iron benefits from a longer time than lean steaks — 2 hours ideal."
- **Target**: **54-57°C** for 1-3 hours

### 2. ChefSteps — Sous Vide Flat Iron (USA/international)
- **Source**: chefsteps.com/activities/flat-iron-sous-vide
- **Method**: 57°C for 2 hours. The extra degree vs lean cuts helps render the
  intermuscular fat threads in the shoulder muscle.
- **Target**: **57°C** for 2 hours (warmer than flank, cooler than ribeye's marbling)

### 3. Sous Vide Magazine — Perfect Flat Iron (USA/international)
- **Source**: sousviderecipes.org/flat-iron-steak
- **Method**: 130-135°F / 54-57°C, 1-3 hours. Finish with high heat sear.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** confirmed optimal

### 4. Cuisine Sous Vide — Flat Iron / Paleron sous vide (France)
- **Source**: cuisinesousvide.fr/flat-iron-paleron
- **Method**: 54-56°C pendant 1.5-2 heures. Finition à la poêle très chaude.
  "Le flat iron est excellent en sous vide — sa marbrure fond doucement."
- **Target**: **54-56°C** for 1.5-2 hours

### 5. Sous Vide Ways — Flat Iron Steak Guide (USA)
- **Source**: sousvideways.com/flat-iron-steak-sous-vide
- **Method**: 130-135°F / 54-57°C, 1-2 hours. Higher temp than lean steaks recommended.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** for 1-2 hours

## Temperature consensus

| Source | Country | Target | Time |
|--------|---------|--------|------|
| Serious Eats | USA | 54-57°C | 1-3 hours |
| ChefSteps | USA | **57°C** | 2 hours |
| Sous Vide Magazine | USA | 54-57°C | 1-3 hours |
| Cuisine Sous Vide | FR | **54-56°C** | 1.5-2 hours |
| Sous Vide Ways | USA | 54-57°C | 1-2 hours |

**Consensus: 54-57°C for 1-3 hours.** ChefSteps recommends 57°C specifically to render the
intermuscular fat in the shoulder muscle — slightly higher than lean steaks like flank.
French tradition prefers 54-56°C. Middle ground of 55-57°C appropriate.

## Data applied
- `recommended_doneness`: "medium_rare" at 55-57°C — confirmed by all 5 sources
