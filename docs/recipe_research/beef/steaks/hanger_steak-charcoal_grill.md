# Hanger Steak × Charcoal Grill — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Charcoal hanger steak is especially prized for the smoke flavour it picks up — the
mineral, iron-rich flavour of hanger is amplified by charcoal. French tradition
(onglet sur braise) is the reference here.

## Source recipes

### 1. Serious Eats — Charcoal-Grilled Hanger Steak (USA)
- **Source**: seriouseats.com/charcoal-grilled-hanger-steak
- **Method**: Hardwood charcoal, direct heat. Sinew removed. 4-5 min/side.
  Rest 5-7 min. Slice against grain.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. La Bonne Cuisine — Onglet sur braise (France)
- **Source**: labonnecuisine.fr/onglet-braise
- **Method**: Braise de bois/charbon. Gril chaud. 3-4 min/côté. Reposer 5 min.
  "L'onglet sur braise avec des échalotes confites — la quintessence du bistrot français."
- **Target**: **52-55°C** (saignant — non-négociable pour l'onglet)

### 3. ChurrascoBrasil — Fraldão na Brasa (Brazil)
- **Source**: churrascosbrasil.com.br/fraldao-brasa
- **Method**: Brasa viva, direto. 3-4 min/lado para corte fino.
- **Target**: **54-58°C** (ao ponto mal passado)

### 4. Gastro — Hanger Steak på kulgrill (Denmark)
- **Source**: gastro.dk/hanger-steak-kulgrill
- **Method**: Kulgrill direkte. 4-5 min per side. Hvil 5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare)

### 5. Braaiboy — Hanger Steak on Charcoal (South Africa)
- **Source**: braaiboy.co.za/hanger-steak-charcoal
- **Method**: Charcoal braai, direct heat. 4-5 min/side. Rest.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

## Temperature consensus

| Source | Country | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Serious Eats | USA | 54-57°C |
| La Bonne Cuisine | FR | **52-55°C** (saignant) |
| ChurrascoBrasil | BR | 54-58°C |
| Gastro | DK | 54-57°C |
| Braaiboy | ZA | 54-57°C |

**Consensus: 54-57°C medium-rare. French insists on 52-55°C saignant for onglet.**
Charcoal = same target as gas grill.
