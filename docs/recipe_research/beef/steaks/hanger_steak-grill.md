# Hanger Steak × Grill (gas) — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Grilled hanger steak has a long tradition in the USA (steakhouses call it "butcher's
steak"). Gas grill direct heat works well for this thin to medium-thick cut.
Remove the central sinew before grilling — it contracts and distorts the steak.

## Source recipes

### 1. Bon Appétit — Grilled Hanger Steak (USA)
- **Source**: bonappetit.com/recipe/grilled-hanger-steak
- **Method**: Gas grill 230°C+. Direct heat. 4-5 min/side. Rest 5-7 min. Slice against grain.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. Food & Wine — Grilled Hanger Steak with Herb Butter (USA)
- **Source**: foodandwine.com/recipe/grilled-hanger-steak-herb-butter
- **Method**: Gas grill high direct heat. 4-5 min/side. Let rest. Herb butter finish.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 3. ICA Recept — Grillad onglet / hängarbiff (Sweden)
- **Source**: ica.se/recept/grillad-onglet
- **Method**: Gasgrill direkt hög värme. 4-5 min per sida. Vila 5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

### 4. BBC Good Food — Grilled Hanger Steak with Béarnaise (UK)
- **Source**: bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/grilled-hanger-steak-bearnaise
- **Method**: Gas grill maximum heat. 3-4 min/side. Rest 5 min. Béarnaise or
  chimichurri accompaniment.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

### 5. Grillen mit Leidenschaft — Hanger Steak vom Gasgrill (Germany)
- **Source**: grillenundfleisch.de/hanger-steak-gasgrill
- **Method**: Gasgrill volle Hitze. Direkte Hitze. 4-5 Min pro Seite. Ruhe 5 Min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / rosa)
