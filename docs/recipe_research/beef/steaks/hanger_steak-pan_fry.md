# Hanger Steak × Pan Fry — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Pan frying hanger steak is essentially the same as pan searing — the cut is thin
enough that the distinction is minimal. French onglet tradition dominates this method.

## Source recipes

### 1. AllRecipes — Pan-Fried Hanger Steak (USA, 4.7★)
- **Source**: allrecipes.com/recipe/pan-fried-hanger-steak
- **Method**: Medium-high heat, oil. 3-4 min/side after removing sinew. Rest 5 min.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. 750g — Onglet de bœuf à la poêle (France)
- **Source**: 750g.com/recettes/onglet-boeuf-poele
- **Method**: Poêle très chaude. Beurre + ail + thym. 2-3 min/côté. Reposer 5 min.
- **Target**: **52-55°C** (saignant — French standard for onglet)
- "C'est le morceau préféré des bouchers eux-mêmes."

### 3. RecipeTin Eats — Pan Fried Hanger Steak (Australia)
- **Source**: recipetineats.com/pan-fried-hanger-steak
- **Method**: Pat dry, season. Oil, high heat. 3-4 min/side. Rest 5 min. Slice against
  the grain.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

### 4. Kogebogen — Onglet i pande (Denmark)
- **Source**: kogebogen.dk/onglet-pande
- **Method**: Pande meget varm, smør. 3 min per side. Hvil 5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / blodig)

### 5. Olivia's Cuisine — Hanger Steak Pan Fry (USA/Brazil)
- **Source**: oliviascuisine.com/hanger-steak-pan-fry
- **Method**: Cast iron, high heat. 3-4 min/side. Chimichurri sauce recommended.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare
