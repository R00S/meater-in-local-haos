# New York Strip × Air Fryer — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
NY strip in an air fryer at 200°C / 400°F: 360° circulating air creates even browning.
For a cut with a fat cap, the air fryer is slightly less effective than cast iron at
rendering the fat edge (no direct metal contact) — but the overall interior result
is excellent. Pull at 52-54°C, rest to 54-57°C.

## Source recipes

### 1. The Kitchenly — How Long to Cook New York Strip in Air Fryer
- **Source**: thekitchenly.com/how-long-to-cook-new-york-strip-in-air-fryer
- **Method**: 400°F / 200°C, preheat 3-5 min. Flip at halfway (5-6 min).
- **Time for 1-inch steak (medium-rare)**: 9-12 min total
- **Target**: Pull at **125-130°F / 52-54°C**, rest to **130-135°F / 54-57°C**

### 2. AllRecipes — Air Fryer New York Strip Steak Recipe
- **Source**: allrecipes.com/air-fryer-new-york-strip-steak-recipe-7568496
- **Method**: 400°F / 200°C, flip halfway.
- **Target**: Medium-rare = **130-135°F / 54-57°C** internal after rest.

### 3. Air Frying Foodie — Air Fryer New York Strip Steak
- **Source**: airfryingfoodie.com/air-fryer-new-york-strip-steak
- **Method**: Preheat air fryer to 400°F / 200°C. Pat dry, thin oil coat.

- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 4. Lara Clevenger — Air Fryer New York Strip Steaks

- **Target**: **130°F / 54°C** internal after 5-min rest

- **Source**: shortfoodblog.com/how-do-i-cook-a-medium-rare-new-york-strip-in-an-air-fryer
