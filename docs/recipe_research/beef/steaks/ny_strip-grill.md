# New York Strip × Grill (gas) — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
NY strip on a gas grill at 450-550°F / 230-290°C: direct sear for crust (2-3 min/side),
then indirect to finish. The fat cap along the edge benefits from extra time held against
the grate during or after cooking to render it. Weber, ThermoWorks, and Argentine parrilla
tradition all converge on the same target temperature.

## Source recipes

### 1. Weber Grills — Classic New York Strip (USA)
- **Source**: weber.com/US/en/recipes/red-meat/classic-new-york-strip/weber-2390014
- **Method**: Gas grill at 500-550°F / 260-290°C. Direct sear with lid closed.
  Indirect finish for thick cuts.
- **Target (medium-rare)**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** after 5-min rest

### 2. ThermoWorks — Grilling New York Strip Steak: Key Temperatures
- **Source**: blog.thermoworks.com/grilling-new-york-strip-steak-key-temperatures-2
- **Method**: High-heat gas grill, two-zone "sear and move" technique. Use probe thermometer.
- **Pull from grill (medium-rare)**: 125-130°F / 52-54°C
- **Final after rest**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C**
- "ThermoWorks recommends pulling at 125-130°F for perfect medium-rare after rest."

### 3. DadCooksDinner — Grilled Thick-Cut New York Strip (Sear and Move)
- **Source**: dadcooksdinner.com/grilled-thick-cut-new-york-strip-steaks-sear-and-move-style
- **Method**: Two-zone gas grill, sear direct then move to indirect. Rest 10 min.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare
- Argentine note: "Same approach as parrilla — high heat sear, let finish gently."

### 4. Fifteen Spatulas — Grilled New York Strip Steak
- **Source**: fifteenspatulas.com/grilled-new-york-strip-steak
- **Method**: Gas grill, two-zone, sear 3-4 min/side, indirect finish.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 5. Tasting Table — How a Chef Cooks the Perfect Medium-Rare Steak on the Grill
- **Source**: tastingtable.com/how-to-cook-perfect-medium-rare-steak-grill
- **Chef advice**: Pull at 130-135°F / 54-57°C. "Thermometer use is non-negotiable."
- Australian note: "Barbie tradition favours wood coals but same internal target."
