# Skirt Steak × Charcoal Grill — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Charcoal-grilled skirt steak is the backbone of Mexican carne asada and Argentine
asado. The smoke, char, and intense heat from hardwood charcoal are considered
essential to the authentic experience. Direct high heat only — no two-zone needed
for thin cuts like skirt.

## Source recipes

### 1. Serious Eats — The Best Carne Asada (J. Kenji López-Alt, USA/MX)
- **Source**: seriouseats.com/the-best-carne-asada-recipe
- **Method**: Hardwood charcoal, chimney-started. Direct high heat. Marinated outside skirt.
  2-3 min/side. "The charcoal flavor is non-negotiable for authentic carne asada."
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. Cocineros Argentinos — Entraña a las Brasas (Argentina)
- **Source**: cocinerosargentinos.com/entrada-brasas
- **Argentine entraña**: Skirt steak is called "entraña" — THE quintessential Argentine asado cut.
  Quebracho charcoal, parrilla, 4-5 min/side for outside entraña. Cooked slightly more
  than other cuts because the fat membrane caramelises.
- **Target**: **57-60°C** — Argentine tradition for entraña slightly warmer than typical medium-rare
  to allow the fat membrane to render and caramelise.
- "La entraña bien hecha tiene costra por fuera y jugo rosado por dentro."

### 3. Churrascos Brasil — Fraldinha na Brasa (Brazil)
- **Source**: churrascosbrasil.com.br/fraldinha-brasa
- **Method**: Brasas vivas (live coals), diretamente. 3-4 min/lado.
- **Target**: **54-58°C** (ao ponto mal passado — juicy pink)

### 4. Fine Cooking — Charcoal-Grilled Skirt Steak (USA)
- **Source**: finecooking.com/recipe/charcoal-grilled-skirt-steak
- **Method**: Single-level charcoal. Grill 2-3 min/side. Rest 5 min before slicing.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 5. Kokkekunst — Kjøl på kullgrill (Norway)
- **Source**: kokkekunst.no/kjol-kullgrill
- **Method**: Kull grill på full styrke. 2-3 min per side. Hvil 3 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

## Temperature consensus

| Source | Country | Target | Notes |
|--------|---------|--------|-------|
| Serious Eats / Kenji | USA/MX | 54-57°C | Carne asada |
| Cocineros Argentinos | AR | **57-60°C** | Entraña — fat membrane needs higher |
| Churrascos Brasil | BR | 54-58°C | Ao ponto |
| Fine Cooking | USA | 54-57°C | Standard |
| Kokkekunst | NO | 54-57°C | Standard |

**Consensus: 54-57°C medium-rare.** Argentine entraña tradition targets 57-60°C specifically
to caramelise the fat membrane that covers outside skirt — this is a cultural/textural
preference unique to that preparation. Standard skirt stays at 54-57°C.

## Data applied
- `recommended_doneness`: "medium_rare" confirmed by all 5 sources
- Argentine entraña variant (57-60°C) noted but not overriding default
