# Skirt Steak × Grill (gas) — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Grilled skirt steak is iconic — Mexican carne asada, Brazilian churrasco, US fajitas
all use the grill. The high heat chars the outside while keeping the thin cut juicy
inside. Gas grill works well with direct high heat.

## Source recipes

### 1. Weber — Grilled Skirt Steak Fajitas (USA)
- **Source**: weber.com/us/en/recipes/beef/skirt-steak-fajitas
- **Method**: Gas grill 260°C+ direct. 3-4 min/side for outside skirt. Rest 5 min.
  Slice across grain on bias for fajita strips.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. Bon Appétit — Carne Asada (USA/MX tradition)
- **Source**: bonappetit.com/recipe/the-best-carne-asada
- **Method**: Gas or charcoal grill maximum heat. Marinate 2-24 hr (lime, garlic, cumin).
  2-3 min/side for outside skirt. Slice thin on bias.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare
- "Carne asada should be pink in the middle — charred outside, juicy inside."

### 3. ICA Sverige — Grillad Arrachera / Kjölbiff (Sweden)
- **Source**: ica.se/recept/grillad-arrachera
- **Method**: Gasgrill hög värme. 2-3 min per sida. Vila 5 min. Skär mot fibrerna.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

### 4. Giallo Zafferano — Bistecca di Diaframma alla Griglia (Italy)
- **Source**: giallozafferano.it/ricetta/bistecca-diaframma-griglia
- **Method**: Griglia a gas alta temperatura, 2-3 min per lato. Riposo 3-5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (al sangue/rosé)

### 5. Gourmand South Africa — Skirt Steak on the Gas Grill
- **Source**: gourmandsa.co.za/skirt-steak-gas-grill
- **Method**: Gas braai/grill max heat. 3 min/side. Rest 5 min. Slice thinly.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

## Temperature consensus

| Source | Country | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Weber Grills | USA | 54-57°C |
| Bon Appétit (carne asada) | USA/MX | 54-57°C |
| ICA Sverige | SE | 54-57°C |
| Giallo Zafferano | IT | 54-57°C |
| Gourmand SA | ZA | 54-57°C |

**Consensus: 54-57°C medium-rare.** Universal agreement across all traditions.
Gas grill gives good char without the complexity of managing charcoal — same target.
