# Skirt Steak × Pan Fry — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Pan fry for skirt steak uses slightly more oil and sometimes a longer cook than pure
searing. The cut is thin so the difference is minimal. Mexican fajita strips and
Asian stir-fry applications dominate this method for skirt.

## Source recipes

### 1. AllRecipes — Pan-Fried Skirt Steak Fajitas (USA, 4.7★)
- **Source**: allrecipes.com/recipe/pan-fried-skirt-steak-fajitas
- **Method**: Medium-high heat, oil, 3-4 min/side for whole skirt. Slice into strips.
  "Don't overcook — fajita meat should be pink inside, never grey."
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. Taste of Home — Skirt Steak Fajitas (USA)
- **Source**: tasteofhome.com/recipes/skirt-steak-fajitas
- **Method**: Cast iron medium-high, oil. 3-4 min/side. Rest 3 min. Slice thin.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare
- "The key to tender fajitas: don't overcook and always slice against the grain."

### 3. Cocina con Carmen — Arrachera en sartén (Mexico)
- **Source**: cocinaconcarmen.mx/arrachera-sartenea
- **Method**: Sartén muy caliente, aceite, 2-3 min/lado. Reposar 3 min. Cortar al sesgo.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (término medio). Mexican standard for arrachera.
- "La arrachera es el rey de los tacos de bistec."

### 4. Simply Recipes — Pan-Fried Skirt Steak (USA)
- **Source**: simplyrecipes.com/recipes/pan_fried_skirt_steak
- **Method**: Very hot pan, high smoke point oil. 2-3 min/side for thin outside skirt.
  Inside skirt needs 30 sec more per side.
- **Target**: **130°F / 54°C** — "skirt steak toughens very quickly past this point"

### 5. Odsherreds Kogebog — Pandestegt skørtsteak (Denmark)
- **Source**: odsherredskogebog.dk/pandestegt-skorsteak
- **Method**: Pande meget varm, olie. 2 min per side. Hvil 3 min. Skær på tværs.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

## Temperature consensus

| Source | Country | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| AllRecipes | USA | 54-57°C |
| Taste of Home | USA | 54-57°C |
| Cocina con Carmen | MX | 54-57°C |
| Simply Recipes | USA | **54°C** (lower end) |
| Odsherredskogebog | DK | 54-57°C |

**Consensus: 54-57°C medium-rare.** Simply Recipes calls the lower end specifically — skirt
is so thin it's easy to overshoot. Pan fry = same target as pan sear for this cut.
