# Skirt Steak × Pan Sear — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Skirt steak is from the diaphragm muscle — even more fibrous than flank and with a
stronger, beefier flavour. Two types: inside skirt (tougher) and outside skirt
(preferred — more tender, sold at premium). Mexican fajita tradition uses skirt almost
exclusively. Rules: very high heat, very short time, always slice against grain.

## Source recipes

### 1. Serious Eats — Perfect Pan-Seared Skirt Steak (J. Kenji López-Alt, USA)
- **Source**: seriouseats.com/pan-seared-skirt-steak-recipe
- **Method**: Outside skirt preferred. Cast iron ripping hot. 1-2 min/side MAXIMUM
  for 1cm thick. No resting needed as long — thin cut equilibrates fast. Slice immediately.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare
- "Outside skirt is sold to restaurants — ask your butcher. It's worth it."

### 2. Rick Bayless — Carne Asada (Mexico/USA)
- **Source**: rickbayless.com/recipe/classic-carne-asada
- **Mexican tradition**: Skirt steak (arrachera) is THE fajita/carne asada cut.
  Very hot comal or cast iron. 2-3 min/side. Slice thin across grain immediately.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** — "al punto" in Mexican tradition
- "Arrachera bien hecha — jugosa y rosada, nunca bien cocida."

### 3. Matprat — Stekt kjøl/skjørtbiff (Norway)
- **Source**: matprat.no/oppskrifter/kjott/stekt-skjortbiff
- **Method**: Stekepanne veldig varm. Olje. 2 min per side. Hvil 3 min. Skjær mot fibrene.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

### 4. Le Figaro Cuisine — La jupe de bœuf à la poêle (France)
- **Source**: cuisine.lefigaro.fr/recette/jupe-boeuf-poele
- **French**: "jupe" or "hampe" — similar muscles. Very hot pan, 1-2 min/side.
- **Target**: **52-55°C** saignant — French always prefer the rarer end for this cut.
- "La jupe grillée est un morceau populaire dans la cuisine populaire française."

### 5. The Woks of Life — Chinese-American Stir-Fry Skirt Steak (USA/CN)
- **Source**: thewoksoflife.com/skirt-steak-stir-fry
- **Pan sear note**: Even in stir-fry applications, skirt targets 54-57°C as a finishing temp
  before sauce is added. High wok heat, quick sear, then sauce finish.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** — same target regardless of cuisine context

## Temperature consensus

| Source | Country | Target | Style |
|--------|---------|--------|-------|
| Serious Eats | USA | 54-57°C | Scientific |
| Rick Bayless | MX/USA | 54-57°C | Arrachera/fajita |
| Matprat | NO | 54-57°C | Medium blodig |
| Le Figaro | FR | **52-55°C** | Saignant |
| Woks of Life | USA/CN | 54-57°C | Any cuisine |

**Consensus: 54-57°C medium-rare.** Even harder ceiling here than flank — skirt is so
thin and fibrous that it goes from perfect to shoe-leather very fast. French 52-55°C.
