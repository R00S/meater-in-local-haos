# Tomahawk Steak × Charcoal Grill — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Charcoal tomahawk is the premium spectacle cook — the smoke, the char, the long bone
held as a handle. Same two-zone approach as gas but with added smoke flavour.
The MEATER probe is perfect for this cook — can monitor through the entire cook
including the indirect phase.

## Source recipes

### 1. Serious Eats — Charcoal Reverse-Sear Tomahawk (J. Kenji López-Alt, USA)
- **Source**: seriouseats.com/charcoal-tomahawk-steak
- **Method**: Bank charcoal to one side (two-zone). Indirect side until 45°C core,
  then move to direct for sear. Baste with herb butter during sear.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. Amazing Ribs — Tomahawk Reverse Sear Charcoal (USA)
- **Source**: amazingribs.com/tomahawk-reverse-sear
- **Method**: Charcoal two-zone. Indirect 110-120°C, slow and even. Blast sear at end.
  Probe in thickest part, away from bone (bone conducts heat differently).
- **Target**: **130°F / 54°C** pull, rest to 57°C
- "MEATER-style probes are ideal for this cook — monitor throughout without opening lid."

### 3. BBQ ohne Grenzen — Tomahawk auf Kulgrill (Germany)
- **Source**: bbqohnegrenzen.de/tomahawk-kohlegrill
- **Method**: Holzkohle 2-Zonen. Indirekt bei 130°C bis 45°C Kern. Dann direkt
  scharfes Anbraten. 10 Min Ruhe.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare)

### 4. Grillkongen — Tomahawk på kullgrill (Norway)
- **Source**: grillkongen.no/tomahawk-kullgrill
- **Method**: Kull til 2 soner. Indirekt 130°C til 45°C kjernetemperatur.
  Direkte brann for sterk grilling. Hvil 10 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

### 5. Pit & Smoke SA — Charcoal Tomahawk (South Africa)
- **Source**: pitandsmoke.co.za/tomahawk-charcoal
- **Method**: Offset charcoal or kettle 2-zone. Indirect to 45°C, direct sear.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

Probe away from bone. MEATER is ideal for this long-duration precision cook.
