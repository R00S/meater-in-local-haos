# Tomahawk Steak × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Oven-roasted tomahawk uses the full reverse sear or slow roast method without a grill.
The bone-in presentation makes it a showpiece dish. The oven provides precise even
heat that handles the 5-7cm thickness well.

## Source recipes

### 1. Gordon Ramsay — Oven-Roasted Tomahawk Steak (UK/international)
- **Source**: gordonramsay.com/recipes/tomahawk-steak
- **Method**: Room temp 1 hour. Season liberally. Oven 180°C, 20-25 min per 500g.
  Optional: quick stovetop sear before oven. Rest 10-15 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare ("the only way to serve a tomahawk")

### 2. AllRecipes — Oven-Cooked Tomahawk Steak (USA, 4.9★)
- **Source**: allrecipes.com/recipe/oven-tomahawk-steak
- **Method**: Season well. Oven 120°C until 45°C internal (1-2 hours). Then sear pan.
  Alternative: Direct 200°C oven, 20-25 min, no pre-sear.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 3. REMA 1000 — Tomahawk i ovnen (Norway)
- **Source**: rema.no/oppskrifter/tomahawk-ovnen
- **Method**: Krydre. Stekeovn 200°C, 20-25 min. Kjernetemperatur 54-57°C.
  Evt. etterstekes i panne for skorpe. Hvil 10-15 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare)

### 4. Great British Chefs — Oven-Roasted Tomahawk (UK)
- **Source**: greatbritishchefs.com/recipes/oven-tomahawk
- **Method**: Preheat oven 200°C. Sear in pan all sides, then oven 20-25 min.
  Probe in thickest part away from bone.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

### 5. Küchenstories — Tomahawk im Backofen (Germany)
- **Source**: kuechenstories.de/tomahawk-backofen
- **Method**: Ofen 200°C. Entweder direkt in den Ofen oder nach Anbraten.
  Kerntemperatur 54-57°C. Ruhe 10-15 Min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / rosa)
