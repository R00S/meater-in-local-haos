# Tomahawk × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile

The tomahawk oven roast uses the reverse-sear method: slow oven first at low temperature to cook the interior evenly, then a hot sear (on the stove or under the grill) for the crust. Because of the 5–8 cm thickness, cooking it entirely on the stove is impossible — the exterior would be cremated before the centre is safe. At 90 °C in the oven, the steak takes 90 minutes for medium; at 140 °C (fan) it takes 40–45 minutes. Both yield excellent, evenly-cooked results with minimal grey-band.

Target internal temperatures (oven pull temps before sear):
- For medium-rare: **pull from oven at 50 °C (122 °F)**, sear to 55 °C, rest to **57 °C (135 °F)**

## Styckesprofil

Tomahawk ugnsrost använder reverse-sear-metoden: långsam ugn först vid låg temperatur för att tillaga insidan jämnt, sedan en het bryning (på spisen eller under grillen) för skorpan. På grund av 5–8 cm tjockleken är det omöjligt att tillaga den helt på spisen — ytan skulle kremeras innan centrumet når temperatur. Ugnen ger den nödvändiga milda, jämna värmen för kärnan medan den avslutande bryningen ger den karamelliserade skorpan. Benet fungerar som en naturlig basningspinne under vila.

Målinterna temperaturer:
- Medium-rare: **dra från ugn vid 48–50 °C (118–122 °F)**, vila 15 min, sedan bryna till **55–57 °C (131–135 °F)**
- Medium: **dra från ugn vid 55–57 °C (131–135 °F)**, vila till **62–63 °C (144–145 °F)**
## Source recipes

### 1. Chefkoch.de — Tomahawk-Steak im Backofen, rückwärts gegart (German tradition)
**Source**: 0602, Chefkoch.de; https://www.chefkoch.de/rezepte/3007701453644532/Tomahawk-Steak-im-Backofen.html (fetched 2026-04-24). *"Das Steak ist butterzart und das Beste, welches ich jemals gegessen habe."*
**Serves**: 2 (or 3–4 with more sides) · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 1 hr 40 min oven + 3–4 min sear · **Rating**: 4.9/5 (16 ratings)

*Translation: Tomahawk steak in the oven, reverse-cooked. "The steak is butter-tender and the best I have ever eaten."*

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**: "90 Minuten garen, dann ist es medium (Handballentest Daumen/Mittelfinger)." (90 minutes at 90 °C = medium by the thumb-middle-finger palm test.)

**Ingredients**
- 1 tomahawk steak, approx. 5 cm thick
- Oil for coating
- Coarse sea salt or salt flakes
- Steak pepper

**Method**
1. Remove tomahawk from refrigerator; bring to room temperature.
2. Preheat oven to **90 °C Ober-/Unterhitze** (upper/lower heat — no fan). Rub steak with oil, salt, and steak pepper.
3. Place a little oil on a baking sheet; lay steak on it. Cook on the **middle rack for 90 minutes** for medium. Turn once halfway through.
4. Remove from oven. Carefully scrape off the spices.
5. Heat a heavy pan with oil until smoking hot. Sear steak **1.5–2 minutes per side** to develop a caramelised crust.
6. Insert MEATER probe before the sear step. **Pull at 52–55 °C (126–131 °F)** for medium.
7. A salad is sufficient as a side for 2. With more sides (chips, vegetables) it feeds 3–4.
*Final resting temperature: 54–58 °C (129–136 °F).*

### 2. RecipeTin Eats — Tomahawk Steak (oven phase of reverse sear) (Australian tradition)
**Source**: Nagi Maehashi & JB, RecipeTin Eats; https://www.recipetineats.com/tomahawk-steak/ (fetched 2026-04-24). *Reverse-sear method: low oven first to cook interior evenly, then high-heat grill or broiler for the crust.*
**Serves**: 2–3 · **Prep**: 10 min (de-chill 1 hr) · **Cook**: 40–45 min oven + 6–8 min sear

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**: "Roast at 140 °C/285 °F (120 °C fan) for 40–45 minutes until 50 °C/122 °F." After BBQ sear: 55–57 °C (131–135 °F) for perfect medium-rare.

**Doneness chart (from recipe)**:
| Doneness | Pull from oven | Pull from sear | After rest |
|----------|---------------|----------------|------------|
| Medium-rare | 50 °C (122 °F) | 55 °C (131 °F) | 57 °C (135 °F) |
| Medium | 55 °C (131 °F) | 60 °C (140 °F) | 62 °C (144 °F) |
| Medium-well | 60 °C (140 °F) | 65 °C (149 °F) | 67 °C (153 °F) |

**Ingredients**
- 1 tomahawk steak, 1.2–1.5 kg
- Neutral oil
- Salt and pepper

**Method**
1. De-chill 1 hour; season just before cooking (not ahead — avoids surface moisture).
2. Place on rack over baking tray. Roast at **140 °C (120 °C fan) for 40–45 minutes** until internal reaches 50 °C.
3. Rest on counter 10 minutes (internal rises to 53 °C). Surface will be pale — normal.
4. **Finish under broiler (no BBQ option)**: Position rack 10 cm from grill element. Broil 4–5 minutes on each side for crust if no BBQ available.
5. Insert MEATER probe. **Pull from oven at 50 °C (122 °F)** for medium-rare.
6. Rest 3 minutes; serves at 57 °C (135 °F).
*Final resting temperature: 57–60 °C (135–140 °F).*

### 3. GialloZafferano — Tomahawk alla griglia (Italian oven-finish variant)
**Source**: GialloZafferano; https://ricette.giallozafferano.it/Tomahawk-alla-griglia.html (fetched 2026-04-24). *Italian tomahawk technique: grilled then rested covered with foil in a warm spot — equivalent to a warm-oven rest finish.*
**Serves**: 2–4 · **Prep**: 1 hr tempering · **Cook**: 30 min grill + 10 min warm rest

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**: "La temperatura al cuore deve raggiungere i 55°–60°." Cover with foil and rest 10 minutes in a warm place after grilling.

**Ingredients**
- 1 tomahawk steak, 4–8 cm thick
- Aromatic salt: garlic, juniper berries, black peppercorns, coarse salt, clarified butter, bay leaves, sage, rosemary (blended to a paste)

**Method**
1. Leave steak at room temperature for at least 1 hour.
2. Rub aromatic salt paste all over all sides.
3. Grill 10 minutes per side over direct heat, managing flare-ups. Rotate 90° for cross-hatch marks for additional 5 minutes each side.
4. Insert MEATER probe. **Pull when core reaches 53 °C (127 °F)**.
5. Transfer to a rack over a baking tray. Cover with foil. Rest in a warm place (or oven at 60 °C) for **10 minutes** — the key oven-finish step.
6. Temperature rises to 55–60 °C (131–140 °F) during rest.
7. Serve with bone upright.
*Final resting temperature: 55–60 °C (131–140 °F).*
