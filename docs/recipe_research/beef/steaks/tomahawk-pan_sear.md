# Tomahawk Steak × Pan Sear — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Tomahawk is a thick-cut (5-7cm) bone-in ribeye with the long rib bone intact.
The extreme thickness means pan sear alone is insufficient — must use reverse sear
(oven first, sear last) or sear-then-oven. The generous marbling is identical to
ribeye. Probe MUST be inserted through the thick meat, away from the bone.

## Source recipes

### 1. Serious Eats — Reverse-Seared Tomahawk Steak (J. Kenji López-Alt, USA)
- **Source**: seriouseats.com/reverse-sear-tomahawk-steak
- **Method**: Reverse sear essential for 5cm+ thickness. Oven 120°C to 45°C internal,
  then screaming hot cast iron 2-3 min/side. Baste with butter, garlic, thyme.
  Rest 5-10 min.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare
- "A thick tomahawk pan-seared without reverse sear will be grey-band heaven and raw center."

### 2. Salt Bae / Nusr-Et — Tomahawk Pan Sear (Turkey/international)
- **Source**: saltbae.com/tomahawk-steak-technique
- **Method**: Salt crusted. Sear on bone edges first, then both flat sides. Baste
  continuously with butter. Total 15-20 min for 5cm thick.
- **Target**: **57°C** (medium-rare, slightly higher to ensure even cook through thickness)

### 3. Hemkök — Tomahawk i stekpanna (reverse) (Sweden)
- **Source**: hemkok.se/tomahawk-stekpanna
- **Method**: Ugn 100°C tills 45°C kärna. Stekpanna MYCKET het, smör + vitlök + timjan.
  2-3 min per sida. Vila 5 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

### 4. GQ Food — Tomahawk Pan Method (UK)
- **Source**: gq-magazine.co.uk/food/tomahawk-pan-sear
- **Method**: Room temp 1 hour. Sear all edges on bone, then flat sides 3 min each.
  Oven 200°C 10-15 min finish if needed. Rest 10 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

### 5. Steak Locker — Tomahawk Stovetop Guide (UK/international)
- **Source**: steaklocker.com/tomahawk-stovetop
- **Method**: Reverse sear method — oven to 45°C, finish cast iron ripping hot.
  Butter baste throughout finish stage.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

## Temperature consensus

| Source | Country | Target | Method detail |
|--------|---------|--------|---------------|
| Serious Eats | USA | 54-57°C | Reverse sear mandatory |
| Salt Bae/Nusr-Et | TR | **57°C** | Baste-heavy method |
| Hemkök | SE | 54-57°C | Reverse sear |
| GQ Food | UK | 54-57°C | Sear + oven |
| Steak Locker | UK | 54-57°C | Reverse sear |

**Consensus: 54-57°C medium-rare.** Tomahawk requires REVERSE SEAR due to extreme thickness.
Pan sear alone without oven will produce raw center. Probe in center of meat, away from bone.
