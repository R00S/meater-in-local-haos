# Tri-Tip × Grill (gas) — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Gas-grilled tri-tip is the second most common method after smoker/charcoal in the
California Santa Maria BBQ tradition. Two-zone setup is important — sear over direct
high heat then finish on indirect. The triangular shape means the thick end takes
longer; probe in the thickest part.

## Source recipes

### 1. Weber — Grilled Tri-Tip Roast (USA)
- **Source**: weber.com/us/en/recipes/beef/grilled-tri-tip-roast
- **Method**: Two-zone gas grill. Sear direct 5 min/side, then indirect 200°C until
  internal temp reached. Rest 10-15 min. Slice in two directions.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 2. Bon Appétit — Gas-Grilled California Tri-Tip (USA/California)
- **Source**: bonappetit.com/recipe/california-tri-tip
- **Method**: Gas grill, 2-zone. High sear then indirect 190°C finish.
  Rub with garlic salt, pepper, onion powder (Santa Maria style).
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare. "California standard."

### 3. ICA Recept — Grillad tri-tip (Sweden)
- **Source**: ica.se/recept/grillad-tri-tip
- **Method**: Gasgrill med direkt och indirekt zon. Bryn direkt 5 min/sida.
  Avsluta indirekt vid 54-57°C kärna. Vila 10 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare / medium blodig)

### 4. AEG Culinary — Tri-Tip on Gas Grill (international)
- **Source**: aeg.com/culinary/tri-tip-gas-grill
- **Method**: Sear all sides over direct heat, finish indirect at 200°C. Probe in
  thickest part. Rest 10 min minimum.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

### 5. Grill Meister — Tri-Tip vom Gasgrill (Germany)
- **Source**: grillmeister.de/tri-tip-gasgrill
- **Method**: Gasgrill 2-Zonen. Scharf direkt anbraten, dann indirekt 195°C.
  Kerntemperatur 54-57°C. Ruhe 10 Min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (medium-rare)
