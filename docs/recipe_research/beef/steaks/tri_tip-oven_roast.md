# Tri-Tip × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Oven-roasted tri-tip is common when outdoor grilling is not possible. The standard
method is stovetop sear followed by oven roast at 190-220°C. The probe goes in the
thickest part of the triangular cut. Pull temperature is critical as it carries over.

## Source recipes

### 1. Allrecipes — Oven-Roasted Tri-Tip (USA, 4.8★ 2000+ reviews)
- **Source**: allrecipes.com/recipe/oven-roasted-tri-tip
- **Method**: Season and let rest. Cast iron sear 4 min/side. Oven 220°C, 15-25 min
  depending on size. Pull at 130°F / 54°C. Rest 10 min (carryover to 57°C).
- **Target**: **Pull at 130°F / 54°C**, serve at 57°C after rest

### 2. The Pioneer Woman — Oven Tri-Tip Roast (USA)
- **Source**: thepioneerwoman.com/food-cooking/recipes/oven-tri-tip
- **Method**: Rub with spices. Sear cast iron. Oven 200°C, 20-30 min to 130°F.
  Rest 10-15 min.
- **Target**: **130-135°F / 54-57°C** medium-rare

### 3. Godtfood — Tri-tip i ovnen (Norway)
- **Source**: godtfood.no/tri-tip-ovnen
- **Method**: Stekes 3 min/side i panne, deretter ovn 200°C til kjernetemperatur.
  Hvil 10 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** kjernetemperatur (medium-rare)

### 4. JamieOliver.com — Roasted Tri-Tip (UK)
- **Source**: jamieoliver.com/recipes/roasted-tri-tip
- **Method**: Quick stovetop sear, oven 200°C until internal temp reached. Rest 10 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** medium-rare

### 5. Chefclub — Tri-tip rôti au four (France)
- **Source**: chefclub.tv/tri-tip-roti-four
- **Method**: Saisir à la poêle 3-4 min/côté. Cuire au four 200°C jusqu'à 54-57°C.
  Reposer 10 min.
- **Target**: **54-57°C** (rosé)
