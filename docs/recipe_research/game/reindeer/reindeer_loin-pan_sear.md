# Reindeer Loin × Pan Sear — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile

See the primary pan sear research for reindeer fillet (*renfilé*) at [`docs/recipe_research/game/reindeer/reindeer_steak-pan_sear.md`](./reindeer_steak-pan_sear.md). The sources documented there — including the Köket.se recipe explicitly using "renfilé" (600 g reindeer fillet, pan-seared and oven-finished at 125°C to 52°C / 126°F; pull at 52°C for rare/medium-rare) and the Kotikokki.net Finnish butter-and-juniper recipe (pull at 52–55°C) — are all specifically for the reindeer fillet/loin cut.

The reindeer loin requires the same pan sear approach: sear in butter or neutral oil over maximum heat for 2–3 minutes per side, then finish in a low oven (100–125°C / 212–257°F) to target temperature. Pull at **52–54°C / 126–129°F** for medium-rare (final serving at 55–57°C / 131–135°F after rest).

## Styckesprofil

Renfilé (*renfilé*) kan också pannstekas, i enlighet med klassiska skandinaviska metoder. Se primär pannsteksresearch för `reindeer_steak-pan_sear.md`. Kokt.se-recept använder 600 g renfilé, pannstekat och ugnsavslutat vid 125 °C till 52 °C internt.

Pannstekningstiden: 3–4 minuter per sida i smör vid hög värme; vila täckt minst 5 minuter. Innertemperatur: **52–56 °C (126–133 °F)** för medium-rare. Eftertillagning: 3–4 °C. Sond insatt längsvis i den tjockaste delen av filén. Skandinavisk tradition föredrar rosa/medium-rare för maximum saftighet.
