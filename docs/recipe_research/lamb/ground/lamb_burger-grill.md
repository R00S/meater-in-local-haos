## Cut profile

Lamb burgers are popular throughout the UK, Australia, New Zealand, the Middle East, and South Asia. Ground lamb's higher fat content means the patties are juicy but can flare on a grill — medium-high indirect heat is recommended. Common additions: garlic, cumin, harissa, feta (stuffed or mixed in), fresh herbs.

Target internal temperature: **74 °C (165 °F)** for safe ground meat.

## Source recipes

### 1. BBC Good Food — Lamb Burgers with Harissa (British/North African tradition)
**Source**: BBC Good Food; https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/lamb-burgers-harissa (web search 2026-04-24)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 15 min · **Cook**: 12 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g ground lamb
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp harissa paste
- 1 small red onion, grated
- Salt and black pepper
- 4 burger buns
- Toppings: tzatziki, sliced tomato, rocket

**Method**
1. Mix lamb, cumin, coriander, garlic, harissa, onion, salt, and pepper gently. Form 4 patties.
2. Preheat grill or BBQ to medium-high. Cook patties 5–6 minutes per side.
3. Insert probe. **Pull at 72–74 °C (162–165 °F)**.
4. Rest 3 minutes in bun.
5. Serve with tzatziki, sliced tomato, and rocket in a toasted bun.
*Final temperature: 74–76 °C (165–169 °F).*

### 2. Africanbites.com — North African Lamb Burger (Moroccan tradition)
**Source**: Africanbites.com; https://www.africanbites.com/ (web search 2026-04-24)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 20 min · **Cook**: 12 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g ground lamb
- 1 tsp harissa
- 1 tsp ras el hanout spice blend
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
- 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
- Salt and pepper
- 4 flatbreads or buns
- Topping: cucumber yoghurt sauce, chopped tomato, red onion

**Method**
1. Mix all ingredients gently. Form 4 patties.
2. Grill over medium-high heat 5–6 min per side.
3. Insert probe. **Pull at 72–74 °C (162–165 °F)**.
4. Serve in flatbread with cucumber yoghurt and salad.
*Final temperature: 74–76 °C (165–169 °F).*

### 3. RecipeTinEats.com — Greek Lamb Burgers (Australian/Greek tradition)
**Source**: RecipeTinEats.com (Nagi Maehashi); https://www.recipetineats.com/greek-lamb-burgers/ (web search 2026-04-24)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 15 min · **Cook**: 10 min

**Ingredients**
- 500 g ground lamb
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp cumin
- 60 g feta cheese, crumbled (mixed in or topping)
- 2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
- Salt and pepper

**Method**
1. Mix lamb with oregano, garlic powder, cumin, half the feta, mint, salt, and pepper. Form 4 patties.
2. Hot grill. Cook 5 minutes per side.
3. Insert probe. **Pull at 72–74 °C (162–165 °F)**.
4. Serve in toasted bun with tzatziki, remaining feta, and Greek salad.
*Final temperature: 74–76 °C (165–169 °F).*
