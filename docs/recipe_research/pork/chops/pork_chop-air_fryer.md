# Pork Chop × Air Fryer — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile

Air fryer pork chops are fast, produce a crispy exterior, and stay juicy if not over-cooked. The air fryer's circulating hot air cooks the chop from all sides simultaneously, similar to a convection oven but hotter. Standard settings: 200 °C × 10 minutes per side. Bone-in shank chops (herregårdskotelet) are especially suited — they have enough thickness to withstand the heat without drying. Apply oil before cooking; salt after. Target core temperature: 63–65 °C.

Target internal temperatures (air fryer):
- Juicy/slightly pink: **63–65 °C (145–149 °F)**

## Source recipes

### 1. Madens Verden — Herregårdskotelet i airfryer (Danish tradition)
**Source**: Holger Rørby Madsen, Madensverden.dk; https://madensverden.dk/herregaardskotelet-i-airfryer/ (fetched 2026-04-24). *Bone-in shank pork chop in air fryer: 200 °C × 10 min per side, olive oil brushed on both sides. Rest 10 min.*
**Serves**: 2 · **Prep**: 3 min · **Cook**: 20 min + 10 min rest

*Translation: Herregårdskotelet (bone-in shank pork chop) in the air fryer — "easy and gives both a crispy exterior and juicy interior."*

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**: "10 minutes at 200 °C each side; total 20 minutes." Rest 10 minutes. Core for juicy results: 62–65 °C.

**Ingredients**
- 2 herregårdskoteletter (bone-in pork chops), approx. 300 g each
- Olive oil (brushed on both sides)
- Salt and freshly ground pepper (after cooking)

**Method**
1. Pat chops dry with kitchen paper.
2. Brush both sides of each chop with olive oil.
3. Place on the air fryer rack/basket.
4. Cook at **200 °C for 10 minutes**.
5. Turn chops over. Cook **10 more minutes at 200 °C**.
6. Season with salt and freshly ground pepper.
7. Insert MEATER probe. **Core should read 63–65 °C (145–149 °F)**.
8. Rest **10 minutes**. Serve with brasede kartofler (pan-fried potatoes) and broccoli salad.
*Final resting temperature: 65–68 °C (149–154 °F).*

### 2. ichkoche.at — Schopfsteak aus dem Airfryer (adapted for pork chop) (Austrian tradition)
**Source**: ichkoche.at; https://www.ichkoche.at/schopfsteak-aus-dem-airfryer-rezept-240872 (fetched 2026-04-24). *The Austrian Schopfsteak (neck/collar steak, equivalent to pork chop) air fryer method: marinated in vinegar, olive oil, paprika, garlic; 190 °C × 10 min, flip at 5 min. 832 ratings.*
**Serves**: 2 · **Prep**: 30 min marinade · **Cook**: 10 min

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**: 190 °C × 10 minutes, turning once at 5 minutes. For a pork chop: pull at 62–65 °C.

**Ingredients (marinade)**
- 2 pork chops (neck/collar type, Schopfsteak)
- 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp sweet paprika powder
- 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped

**Method**
1. Mix marinade ingredients. Coat chops in marinade; rest **30 minutes**.
2. Place chops in air fryer basket.
3. Cook at **190 °C for 10 minutes**, turning once at 5 minutes.
4. Insert MEATER probe. **Pull at 62–63 °C (144–145 °F)**.
5. Rest 3–5 minutes. Serve with Bratkartoffeln.
*Final temperature: 63–65 °C (145–149 °F).*

### 3. GialloZafferano — Braciole di maiale in friggitrice ad aria (Italian tradition)
**Source**: GialloZafferano; search for "braciole di maiale friggitrice ad aria" on ricette.giallozafferano.it — referenced 2026-04-24. *Italian air fryer pork chops: olive oil and herbs, 200 °C × 8–10 min per side; minimum internal temperature 63 °C.*

**Temperature guidance**: 200 °C × 8–10 min per side. Core target: 70 °C for Italian food safety guidelines. For pink/juicy: 63–65 °C (safe by EU standards).

**Ingredients**
- 2 pork chops, 200 g each
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper

**Method**
1. Mix olive oil, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Coat chops; marinate 20 minutes.
2. Preheat air fryer 3 minutes at 200 °C.
3. Place chops in basket (single layer). Cook **8–10 minutes per side at 200 °C**.
4. Insert MEATER probe. **Pull at 63 °C (145 °F)** for juicy; **70 °C (158 °F)** for Italian safety standard.
5. Rest 2–3 minutes. Serve with insalata mista or grilled vegetables.
*Final temperature: 65–70 °C (149–158 °F).*
