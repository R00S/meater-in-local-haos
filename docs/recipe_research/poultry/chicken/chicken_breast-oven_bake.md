## Cut profile

Boneless, skinless chicken breast is an extremely lean cut that cooks quickly and can dry out easily. The key techniques are: pound to even thickness so the whole piece cooks at the same rate; use a hot oven (400–425 °F / 200–218 °C); pull at exactly 165 °F (74 °C) internal; and rest 5–10 minutes. Some recipes brine first (30 min to 2 h in salted water) for extra moisture insurance.

Target internal temperature: **165 °F (74 °C)** — USDA minimum for poultry, fully safe; higher leads to dryness.

## Source recipes

### 1. Skinnytaste / Gina Homolka — Baked Chicken Breast (mayo + panko crust)
**Source**: Gina Homolka, Skinnytaste; https://www.skinnytaste.com/baked-chicken-breast/ (fetched 2026-04-24).
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min + 1–2 h brine · **Cook**: ~25 min · **Rest**: 5 min

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**:
- Oven: **425 °F (218 °C)**
- Internal target: **165 °F (74 °C)** — confirmed with meat thermometer
- Time: approximately **24–28 minutes** (thinner if pounded; add a few minutes at 400 °F)

**Ingredients**
- 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, pounded to even thickness
- **Brine**: ¼ cup Diamond Crystal kosher salt dissolved in 6 cups water; soak 1–2 h then rinse and pat dry
- **Seasoning**: garlic powder, dried parsley, sweet paprika, salt
- **Topping**: thin layer of mayonnaise (locks in moisture), seasoned panko breadcrumbs + shredded Parmesan cheese

**Method**
1. Brine: dissolve ¼ cup kosher salt in 6 cups water. Add chicken breasts; refrigerate 1–2 hours. Remove, rinse, pat dry.
2. Pound the thickest part to an even thickness.
3. Combine seasoning spices; rub over chicken.
4. Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise over the top surface of each breast.
5. Sprinkle panko + Parmesan over the mayonnaise layer.
6. Preheat oven to **425 °F (218 °C)**. Bake until probe reads **165 °F (74 °C)**, about 24–28 minutes.
7. Rest 5 minutes before serving.

### 2. Natasha's Kitchen / Natasha Kravchuk — Baked Chicken Breast
**Source**: Natasha Kravchuk, Natasha's Kitchen; https://natashaskitchen.com/baked-chicken-breast/ (fetched 2026-04-24).
**Serves**: 6 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 18–26 min (by thickness) · **Rest**: 5–10 min

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**:
- Oven: **400 °F (204 °C)**
- Internal target: **165 °F (74 °C)** — measured at thickest point
- Time by thickness (pounded to even thickness):
  - ½-inch thick: **16–18 min**
  - ¾-inch thick: **20–22 min**
  - 1-inch thick: **24–26 min**

**Ingredients**
- 2 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts (~4 medium pieces), pounded to even thickness
- 1½ tsp fine sea salt, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
- 1 tbsp olive oil, avocado oil, or melted butter

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to **400 °F (204 °C)**, rack in center. Pound chicken breasts to even thickness with a meat mallet between plastic wrap.
2. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet, leaving space between pieces.
3. Pat dry; brush all sides with oil.
4. Combine salt, garlic powder, paprika, and pepper; sprinkle over all sides.
5. Bake until internal temp = **165 °F (74 °C)**, about 18–22 min (see thickness chart above). No flipping needed.
6. Optional: broil for a few minutes before done for more color.
7. Rest 5–10 minutes before slicing.

### 3. Jessica Gavin — Baked Chicken Breast (high-heat method)
**Source**: Jessica Gavin (culinary school graduate); https://www.jessicagavin.com/baked-chicken-breast/ (fetched 2026-04-24).
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 14–17 min · **Rest**: 5 min

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**:
- Oven: **425 °F (218 °C)**
- Internal target: **160–165 °F (71–74 °C)**
- Time: **14–17 minutes** (pounded to ¾-inch thickness, any size breast)

**Ingredients**
- 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts (~2–2½ lb total)
- 3 tsp kosher salt, 2 tsp paprika (sweet or smoked), 1½ tsp garlic powder, 1½ tsp onion powder, 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- ¼ cup olive oil

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to **425 °F (218 °C)**. Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil; grease lightly.
2. Combine all spices in a small bowl.
3. Pat each chicken breast dry. Place between plastic sheets; pound to **¾-inch even thickness** with a mallet or rolling pin.
4. Brush olive oil over both sides of each breast; sprinkle seasoning mix evenly on both sides (~½–1 tsp per side).
5. Place on baking sheet; bake until internal = **160–165 °F (71–74 °C)**, about **14–17 minutes** depending on size.
6. Rest 5 minutes before serving.
