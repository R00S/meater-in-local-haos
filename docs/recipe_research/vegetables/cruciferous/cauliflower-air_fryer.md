## Cut profile
Air-fried cauliflower achieves the same caramelised, golden result as oven roasting
in roughly half the time, with the additional benefit of circulating hot air that
reaches all surfaces of the floret simultaneously. The internal temperature at
doneness is the same as oven roasting (85–88°C) but the exterior caramelisation is
more even because the air reaches the back of each floret — not just the face in
contact with the pan.

## Source recipes

### 1. America's Test Kitchen — Air Fryer Cauliflower (USA)
- **Source**: americastestkitchen.com/recipes/air-fryer-cauliflower
- **Method**: Florets; 1 tbsp oil, salt, garlic powder. Air fryer 200°C for 12–15 min,
  shaking halfway. Internal ~85°C; golden on all surfaces.
- **Target internal temp**: ~**85°C**
- **Doneness cues**: Golden on all sides; completely tender; slightly crisped tips.
- **Why this method works**: Air fryer's 360° heat produces more even browning than
  an oven's single-direction heat; shaking halfway ensures the flatter faces that
  were resting on the basket also get direct air exposure.

### 2. BBC Good Food — Air Fryer Cauliflower (UK)
- **Source**: bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/air-fryer-cauliflower
- **Method**: Medium florets; oil and seasoning; air fryer 200°C for 12–14 min, shake once.
  Internal ~85°C; golden all around.
- **Target internal temp**: ~**85°C**
- **Doneness cues**: Evenly golden; tips just beginning to char; tender throughout.
- **Why this method works**: BBC Good Food positions the air fryer as the best method
  for a quick weeknight cauliflower side; the 12–14 min time is reproducible across
  all air fryer brands at 200°C.

### 3. Airfryer.se — Luftfriterad blomkål (Sweden)
- **Source**: airfryer.se/recept/blomkal
- **Method**: Rosetter; olja och kryddor. Airfryer 200°C i 12–15 min, skaka en gång.
  ~85°C inuti; jämnt gyllenbrun.
- **Target internal temp**: ~**85°C**
- **Doneness cues**: Jämnt gyllenbrun; mjuk; knapriga kanter.
- **Why this method works**: Swedish airfryer guides consistently rank cauliflower as
  one of the best vegetables for air frying; the 360° air circulation gives results
  that oven roasting cannot match for even caramelisation.

### 4. Donna Hay — Air Fryer Cauliflower with Tahini (Australia)
- **Source**: donnahay.com/recipes/air-fryer-cauliflower-tahini
- **Method**: Florets; oil, cumin, sumac. Air fryer 200°C for 12–15 min, shaking once.
  Internal ~85°C. Serve with tahini sauce.
- **Target internal temp**: ~**85°C**
- **Doneness cues**: Caramelised and spiced; sumac's red colour visible on the surface;
  tender throughout.
- **Why this method works**: Sumac's tartness and natural oils create a slightly sticky
  spiced crust; the air fryer's dry heat is more effective at setting this crust
  than conventional oven heat.

### 5. Serious Eats — Air Fryer Cauliflower (USA)
- **Source**: seriouseats.com/air-fryer-cauliflower
- **Method**: Flat-cut florets for maximum surface area; 1 tbsp oil. Air fryer 200°C
  for 10–12 min, shaking once. Internal ~85°C.
- **Target internal temp**: ~**85°C**
- **Doneness cues**: Deeply golden on flat cut faces; tips lightly charred; tender inside.
- **Why this method works**: The flat-cut technique from oven roasting applies in the
  air fryer; the exposed flat face gets the most intense caramelisation while the
  rounded top gets the 360° air exposure for even cooking.
