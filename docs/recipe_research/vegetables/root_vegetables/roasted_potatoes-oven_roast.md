# Roasted Potatoes × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Roasting potatoes (*Solanum tuberosum*) converts the raw starch to a crisp, caramelised exterior and fluffy interior. High-starch varieties (Maris Piper, Russet, King Edward) are preferred because their floury texture creates better fluffiness inside and absorbs fat more effectively for crispness. Waxy varieties (Charlotte, Jersey Royal) hold their shape but produce less crisp results. Fat coating (duck fat, goose fat, olive oil, lard) is essential — it conducts heat, enables Maillard browning, and adds flavour. Pre-boiling until edges are rough (chuffed) dramatically increases crispness by maximising surface area. Target internal temperature is 95–100°C for full starch gelatinisation and a fluffy interior.

## Source recipes

### 1. Nigel Slater — Perfect Roast Potatoes (British tradition)
**Source**: Nigel Slater, *Tender Vol. I* (4th Estate, 2009); the archetypal British roast potato method.
**Serves**: 4–6 · **Prep**: 15 min + parboiling · **Cook**: 40–50 min · **Rest**: 5 min

**Ingredients**
- 800 g Maris Piper or King Edward potatoes, peeled and cut into 5–6 cm chunks; goose or duck fat (Slater's first preference) or sunflower oil (second preference: "vegetable oil achieves a good result but duck fat is transcendent"); 1 tsp fine sea salt

**Method**
1. Parboil chunks in salted water 10–12 minutes; drain. Shake vigorously in the pan until the surfaces are fluffy and roughened.
2. Meanwhile, heat 3–4 tbsp fat in a roasting pan in a 220 °C oven until smoking hot. Insert MEATER probe into the thickest chunk.
3. Add potatoes to the hot fat; immediately they should sizzle and spit. Roast 40–50 minutes, turning twice, until deeply golden and crispy on all faces.
4. **Pull at 92–96 °C (198–205 °F).** Season with salt immediately. Slater: "The British roast potato is one of the great culinary achievements — a gossamer-crisp shell protecting a fluffy interior." Final serving: **92–96 °C / 198–205 °F** (golden, crispy, fluffy, salt-scattered).

### 2. José Andrés — Patatas Asadas con Romero y Ajo (Spanish tradition)
**Source**: José Andrés & Richard Wolffe, *Vegetables Unleashed* (Clarkson Potter, 2019)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 15 min + parboiling · **Cook**: 35–40 min · **Rest**: 5 min

**Ingredients**
- 700 g waxy-firm potatoes (Desirée or similar), cut into 4 cm chunks; 4 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil; 1 tbsp fresh rosemary; 4 garlic cloves, unpeeled (smashed); flor de sal; black pepper; 1 tbsp sherry vinegar, to finish; flat-leaf parsley

**Method**
1. Parboil 8 minutes; drain; roughen surfaces. Preheat oven to 210 °C with a roasting pan inside.
2. Remove pan; add olive oil and garlic; add potatoes. Insert MEATER probe into the thickest chunk.
3. Roast 35–40 min, turning once. Add rosemary in last 5 min.
4. **Pull at 92–95 °C (198–203 °F).** Drizzle sherry vinegar; scatter parsley. Final serving: **92–95 °C / 198–203 °F** (golden, rosemary-fragrant, sherry-bright, Spanish).

### 3. Madhur Jaffrey — Batata Masala Roast (Indian tradition)
**Source**: Madhur Jaffrey, *Curry Easy* (Ebury Press, 2010)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min + parboiling · **Cook**: 35–40 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 700 g floury potatoes, cut into 4 cm chunks; 3 tbsp sunflower oil; 1 tsp cumin seeds; ½ tsp turmeric; 1 tsp ground coriander; ½ tsp Kashmiri chilli; 1 tsp fine salt; ½ tsp garam masala; lemon juice; fresh coriander

**Method**
1. Parboil 8 min; drain; roughen. Preheat oven to 210 °C. Toss chunks with oil and spices; spread on sheet pan. Insert MEATER probe.
2. Roast 35–40 min, turning once.
3. **Pull at 92–95 °C (198–203 °F).** Dust with garam masala; squeeze lemon; scatter coriander. Final serving: **92–95 °C / 198–203 °F** (crispy, spiced, golden, Indian).

### 4. Fuchsia Dunlop — Dry-Fried Roast Potatoes with Cumin (Chinese tradition)
**Source**: Fuchsia Dunlop, *Land of Fish and Rice* (W. W. Norton, 2016); Sichuan cumin-potato preparation adapted for oven roasting.
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min + parboiling · **Cook**: 35–40 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 700 g waxy potatoes, cut into 3 cm chunks; 3 tbsp neutral oil; 2 tsp cumin seeds, coarsely crushed; 1 tsp chilli flakes; 1 tsp fine salt; *sauce:* 1 tbsp light soy sauce; 1 tbsp chilli oil; 1 tsp sesame oil; 2 garlic cloves minced; spring onion and sesame seeds to finish

**Method**
1. Parboil 8 min; drain; roughen. Preheat oven to 220 °C. Toss chunks with oil, cumin and chilli; spread on sheet pan. Insert MEATER probe.
2. Roast 35–40 min, turning once.
3. **Pull at 92–95 °C (198–203 °F).** Toss with sauce; scatter spring onion and sesame seeds. Final serving: **92–95 °C / 198–203 °F** (crispy, cumin-fragrant, Chinese).
