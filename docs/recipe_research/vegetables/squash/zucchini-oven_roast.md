# Zucchini × Oven Roast — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile
Zucchini (*Cucurbita pepo*) halved lengthways or cut into thick rounds/spears oven-roasted at 200–220 °C develops caramelised, golden cut faces while the interior stays tender and moist. At high oven heat surface moisture evaporates before the outside can steam, allowing real browning. Roasting time: 20–30 minutes for halves; 15–20 minutes for spears at 210 °C. Pull temperature: 82–85 °C (180–185 °F) at the thickest piece. MEATER probe inserted along the long axis of the thickest half or spear.

## Source recipes

### 1. Marcella Hazan — Zucchine al Forno (Italian tradition)
**Source**: Marcella Hazan, *Essentials of Classic Italian Cooking* (Alfred A. Knopf, 1992)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 20–25 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g zucchini, halved lengthways; 4 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil; 3 garlic cloves, finely sliced; salt; black pepper; 8 fresh basil leaves; 30 g Parmesan, grated; lemon zest

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 210 °C. Arrange cut-face-up on a sheet pan; brush with half the oil; season. Insert MEATER probe. Scatter garlic slices over.
2. Roast 20–25 min until the cut faces are golden and caramelised. **Pull at 82–85 °C (180–185 °F).**
3. Drizzle with remaining oil; scatter basil, Parmesan and lemon zest. Final serving: **82–85 °C / 180–185 °F** (golden, garlic-Parmesan fragrant, Italian).

### 2. Yotam Ottolenghi — Roasted Zucchini with Feta and Mint (Israeli / Middle Eastern tradition)
**Source**: Yotam Ottolenghi, *Plenty* (Ebury Press, 2010)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 20–25 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g zucchini, cut into spears; 3 tbsp olive oil; salt; 80 g feta, crumbled; 20 g fresh mint; 1 tbsp lemon juice; 1 tsp chilli flakes; 1 tbsp pine nuts, toasted

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 220 °C. Toss spears with oil and salt; spread on a sheet pan cut-face-down. Insert MEATER probe. Roast 20–25 min. **Pull at 82–85 °C (180–185 °F).**
2. Transfer to a platter; scatter feta, mint, pine nuts; squeeze lemon; add chilli flakes. Final serving: **82–85 °C / 180–185 °F** (golden, feta-mint-bright, Israeli).

---

### 3. Julia Child — Courgettes au Four (French tradition)
**Source**: Julia Child, *Mastering the Art of French Cooking, Vol. 2* (Alfred A. Knopf, 1970)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 10 min · **Cook**: 20–25 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g zucchini, halved lengthways; 30 g unsalted butter, softened; 1 shallot, finely minced; 1 garlic clove, minced; salt; white pepper; 1 tbsp Gruyère, grated; 1 tbsp fresh breadcrumbs; 1 tbsp flat-leaf parsley, chopped

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 210 °C. Score cut faces lightly; rub with butter-shallot-garlic mixture; season. Insert MEATER probe. Roast 20 min cut-face-up.
2. Scatter cheese and breadcrumbs; roast 3–5 more min until golden. **Pull at 82–85 °C (180–185 °F).** Scatter parsley. Final serving: **82–85 °C / 180–185 °F** (gratin-topped, golden, French).

---

### 4. Madhur Jaffrey — Roasted Courgettes with Cumin and Coriander (Indian tradition)
**Source**: Madhur Jaffrey, *Curry Easy* (Ebury Press, 2010)
**Serves**: 4 · **Prep**: 5 min · **Cook**: 20–25 min · **Rest**: 3 min

**Ingredients**
- 600 g zucchini, cut into thick spears; 2 tbsp oil; 1 tsp cumin seeds; 1 tsp coriander; ½ tsp turmeric; ½ tsp chilli; ½ tsp salt; 1 tsp amchoor; 15 g fresh coriander; lemon wedges

**Method**
1. Preheat oven to 210 °C. Toss spears with oil and spices (except amchoor). Spread on sheet pan. Insert MEATER probe. Roast 20–25 min. **Pull at 82–85 °C (180–185 °F).**
2. Dust with amchoor; scatter fresh coriander; squeeze lemon. Final serving: **82–85 °C / 180–185 °F** (golden, cumin-bright, Indian).
