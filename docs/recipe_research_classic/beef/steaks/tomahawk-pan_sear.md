# Tomahawk × Pan Sear — Recipe Temperature Research

## Cut profile

The tomahawk is too large to fit in a standard frying pan, so "pan sear" for a tomahawk refers to an edge-sear + large skillet or cast-iron flat-griddle technique, typically combined with an oven finish. The bone (30–40 cm) must be positioned outside the pan. A large cast-iron pan (30+ cm) or a paella pan is ideal. The sear creates the Maillard crust; the oven brings the thick interior to temperature safely without overcooking the exterior. The technique is essentially reverse-sear reversed: sear first, oven second.

Target internal temperatures:
- Medium-rare: **pull from oven at 55 °C (131 °F)**, rest to **57–58 °C (135–136 °F)**

## Source recipes

### 1. ichkoche.at — Tomahawk Steak (Austrian tradition)
**Source**: ichkoche.at; https://www.ichkoche.at/tomahawk-steak-rezept-241334 (fetched 2026-04-24). *Austrian method: thin oil coat, sear 90–120 seconds per side in very hot pan, finish in 140 °C oven to core temperature. 74 user ratings.*
**Serves**: 2 · **Prep**: 45 min (tempering) · **Cook**: 35–50 min total

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**: "Medium 55–57 °C, Done 65 °C. Bei dicken Steaks 30–45 Minuten." (Medium 55–57 °C; well done 65 °C. For thick steaks 30–45 minutes in oven.)

**Ingredients**
- 1 tomahawk steak, 800 g–1.5 kg
- 2 tbsp sunflower oil
- Sea salt flakes
- Freshly ground pepper

**Method**
1. Remove steak from refrigerator 45 minutes before cooking; bring to room temperature.
2. Brush thinly with sunflower oil all over.
3. Heat a large pan or grill-pan very hot. Sear the steak **90–120 seconds per side** over maximum heat for a deep caramelised crust. Also sear the fat-cap edge (stand upright).
4. Transfer to oven at **140 °C**. Cook until desired core temperature is reached: **30–45 minutes for a thick steak**.
5. Insert MEATER probe into the thickest part, away from bone. **Pull at 53–55 °C (127–131 °F)** for medium-rare.
6. Rest 5–10 minutes. Slice against the grain into thick strips.
7. Season with sea salt flakes and freshly ground pepper after resting.
*Final resting temperature: 55–58 °C (131–136 °F).*

### 2. Chefkoch.de — Tomahawk-Steak im Backofen, rückwärts gegart (German tradition)
**Source**: 0602, Chefkoch.de; https://www.chefkoch.de/rezepte/3007701453644532/Tomahawk-Steak-im-Backofen.html (fetched 2026-04-24). *The true reverse: oven 90 minutes at 90 °C to medium, then sear 1.5–2 minutes per side in screaming hot pan. 4.9/5 stars.*
**Serves**: 2 · **Cook**: 1 hr 50 min total

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**: 90 minutes at 90 °C oven = medium doneness (palm test: thumb to middle finger). Sear 1.5–2 min per side in very hot pan after.

**Ingredients**
- 1 tomahawk steak, approx. 5 cm thick
- Oil for pan and oven
- Coarse sea salt or salt flakes
- Steak pepper

**Method**
1. Bring steak to room temperature. Rub with oil, salt, and pepper.
2. Preheat oven to **90 °C (Ober-/Unterhitze)**. Oil a baking sheet; lay steak on it.
3. Cook on middle rack for **90 minutes** turning once halfway. This produces a uniformly medium interior.
4. Remove from oven. Scrape spices off the surface carefully.
5. Heat a large heavy pan with oil until smoking. Sear steak **1.5–2 minutes per side** for crust.
6. Insert MEATER probe. **Pull from oven at 52 °C (126 °F)** then sear; final pull from pan at 55 °C (131 °F).
7. Salad as a side for 2; enough for 3–4 with additional sides.
*Final resting temperature: 55–58 °C (131–136 °F).*

### 3. RecipeTin Eats — Tomahawk Steak (finishing method without BBQ) (Australian tradition)
**Source**: Nagi Maehashi & JB, RecipeTin Eats; https://www.recipetineats.com/tomahawk-steak/ (fetched 2026-04-24). *No-BBQ option: low oven first, then oven grill/broiler for sear. Same temperatures as the BBQ version.*
**Serves**: 2–3 · **Cook**: 40–45 min oven + 8–10 min broiler

**Temperature guidance (from recipe)**: Oven 140 °C (fan 120 °C) to 50 °C internal. Broil 10 cm from grill element 4–5 minutes per side. Final rest: 57 °C (135 °F).

**Ingredients**
- 1 tomahawk steak, 1.2–1.5 kg
- Neutral oil
- Salt and pepper

**Method**
1. De-chill 1 hour ahead. Season just before cooking (not ahead).
2. Roast on rack at **140 °C fan for 40–45 minutes** to 50 °C internal.
3. Rest 10 minutes on counter.
4. Position rack 10 cm from broiler/grill element. **Broil 4–5 minutes per side** for crust.
5. Insert MEATER probe through the edge. **Pull at 55 °C (131 °F)**.
6. Rest on rack 3 minutes. Serves at 57 °C (135 °F).
7. Slice 1 cm thick. Serve with Cowboy Butter or steak sauce.
*Final resting temperature: 57–60 °C (135–140 °F).*
