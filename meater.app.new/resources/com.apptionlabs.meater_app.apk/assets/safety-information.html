1. Use MEATER only for its intended purpose.
  2. MEATER is designed for use in meat (food).
  3. To protect against the risk of electric shock, do not immerse the charging block in water or other liquids.
  4. To avoid damage to MEATER, insertion of the probe in the meat past the Safety Notch is necessary.
  5. DO NOT use MEATER probes in microwaves.
  6. DO NOT drop MEATER on a hard surface.
  7. MEATER probes are not dishwasher safe. Please hand wash with soap and water before and after each use.
  8. MEATER is water resistant, not waterproof. Do not submerge in water for long periods of time.
  9. Please take caution with the pointed tip of the probe.
  10. Probe will be very hot during or just after a cook. DO NOT touch with bare hands.
  11. DO NOT submerge the MEATER probe in cold water after cooking. Wait until it has cooled down prior to washing.
  12. MEATER is not a toy. Close supervision is necessary if MEATER is to be used near children.
  13. Make sure the MEATER probes are completely dry when placed back in the charger or MEATER Block.

  17. Avoid applying left/right/up/down pressure on the MEATER ceramic handle when removing from the meat. To remove, pull and twist on the same horizontal axis of insertion.
  18. Consuming raw or undercooked meats, poultry, or seafood may increase your risk of foodborne illness. To reduce the risk, cook your food to USDA safe temperatures.
